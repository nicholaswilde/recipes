# :dumpling: Empanadas

![Empanadas](../assets/images/empanadas.webp){ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 30 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :apple: 2 lbs lean ground beef
- :tea: 1 onion
- :garlic: 3 cloves garlic
- :chestnut: 1 tsp (3 g) cumin
- :chestnut: 0.5 tsp (1 g) coriander
- :candy: 3 Tbsp (29 g) sugar
- :salt: 1 tsp salt
- :salt: 0.5 tsp pepper
- 0.5 tsp accent
- :apple: 0.5 cup (114 g) Dutch soy sauce
- some [empanada pastry][1]

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Brown the lean ground beef with chopped onion, drain and add garlic, cumin, coriander, sugar, salt, pepper, and accent.
Then add Dutch soy sauce.

### Step 2

Make sure meat mixture is cool before you put them in the empanada pastry to fry until golden brown.
