# Butternut Squash Tahini Curry

![Butternut Squash Tahini Curry][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 2 to 3 | 15 to 20 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :olive: 1 Tbsp (14 g) olive oil
- :onion: 1 small yellow onion, diced small
- :curry: 1 Tbsp (15 g) fresh ginger, grated
- :garlic: 1 garlic clove, minced
- :hot_pepper: 1 small red chili pepper, seeded and minced
- :hot_pepper: 1 large red bell pepper, cut into 3/4-inch pieces
- :melon: 1 medium butternut squash, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch cubes (about 3 1/2 cups)
- :curry: 2 Tbsp (30 g) Thai red curry paste
- :stew: 3 cups (710 ml) vegetable broth, plus more as needed
- :tahini: 3 Tbsp (48 g) [tahini][2]
- :stew: 1 Tbsp (15 ml) low-sodium soy sauce or tamari
- :lemon: 1 Tbsp (15 ml) lime juice, freshly squeezed, plus more to taste
- :honey_pot: 1/2 tsp (3 g) maple syrup, or to taste
- :beans: Chickpea croutons (optional)
- :salt: Salt to taste
- :herb: 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish

## :cooking: Cookware

- large saucepan or Dutch oven
- blender

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Heat the **olive oil** in a large saucepan or Dutch oven over medium heat.

### Step 2

Add the **yellow onion** and cook for about 5 to 10 minutes, or until translucent.

### Step 3

Add the **fresh ginger**, **garlic**, and **red chili pepper** and cook for 30 seconds more.

### Step 4

Add the **red bell pepper** and cook for 30 seconds.

### Step 5

Mix in the **butternut squash** and cook for 1 minute more, stirring frequently.

### Step 6

Add the **Thai red curry paste** and cook for 1 more minute, stirring constantly.

### Step 7

Stir in the **vegetable broth** and bring to a gentle boil.

### Step 8

Reduce the heat to a vigorous simmer and cook, covered, for about 5 minutes, or until the squash is tender.

### Step 9

Transfer half of the butternut squash and the liquid to a blender. (Be careful not to transfer the bell pepper pieces.) Blend on high until smooth. Return the blended squash and liquid to the saucepan with the remaining curry, stirring to combine.

### Step 10

Stir in the **tahini**, **soy sauce**, **lime juice**, and **maple syrup**.

### Step 11

Mix in some **chickpea croutons**, if using. Add more **vegetable broth**, depending on the desired consistency. Cook over low heat until warmed through.

### Step 12

Season with **salt** and adjust the lime juice and maple syrup to taste.

### Step 13

Garnish with **fresh cilantro**.
