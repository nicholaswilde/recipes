# :sandwich: Banh Mi Sandwich

![Banh Mi Sandwich](../assets/images/banh-mi-sandwich.webp){ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 6 | 20 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :cheese_wedge: 1 16-oz pkg extra firm tofu
- 1 cup [garlic aioli][1]
- :cupcake: 2 cups [pickled carrot and daikon][2]
- :cucumber: 4 asian cucumber
- :herb: 1 handful cilantro
- :star: 6 french bread

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 skillet

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Grill extra firm tofu on a skillet with some olive oil.

### Step 2

Add [garlic aioli][1], [pickled carrot and daikon][2], asian cucumber, and cilantro to a loaf of french bread and
serve.
