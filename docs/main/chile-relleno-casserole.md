# :bell_pepper: Chile Relleno Casserole

![Chile Relleno Casserole][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8 | 1.25 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

=== "Original"

    - :takeout_box: 2 cups (454 g) half and half
    - :egg: 4 eggs
    - :bread: 0.67 cups (80 g) all-purpose flour
    - :beans: 6 whole green chiles
    - :hot_pepper: 6 4-oz cans diced green chiles (alternative)
    - :orange_circle: 1 lb grated cheddar or jack cheese
    - :glass_of_milk: 1 8-oz can tomato sauce

=== "Modified (Highly Recommended!)"

    - :takeout_box: 2 cups (454 g) half and half
    - :egg: 6 eggs
    - :bread: 0.67 cups (80 g) all-purpose flour
    - :baby_bottle: 0.38 tsp kosher salt (for salting eggs in advance)
    - :glass_of_milk: 6 whole green chiles (split and seeded) or 6 4-oz cans diced green chiles
    - :orange_circle: 1 lb grated cheddar or jack cheese (divided)
    - :apple: Chunky salsa (for serving/garnish)

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 9 x 13 casserole dish
- :page_facing_up: 1 foil

## :pencil: Instructions

=== "Original"

    ### Step 1

    Preheat oven to 350°F.

    ### Step 2

    Beat half and half with eggs and all-purpose flour until smooth.

    ### Step 3

    If using whole green chiles, split open and rinse seeds; drain on paper towel. Use diced green chiles (alternative) as
    an alternative.

    ### Step 4

    Make alternate layers of egg mixture, chiles, and grated cheddar or jack cheese in a 9 x 13 casserole dish.

    ### Step 5

    Pour tomato sauce over top and sprinkle with 1/2 cup of cheese.

    ### Step 6

    Bake uncovered 1.25 hours. Cover with foil if cheese begins to burn.

=== "Modified (Highly Recommended!)"

    ### Step 1

    Preheat oven to 350°F.

    ### Step 2

    Crack 6 eggs into a bowl, add 3/8 tsp kosher salt, and beat. Let the salted eggs rest for at least 15 minutes (J. Kenji López-Alt's method to tenderize the proteins and retain moisture). Whisk in 2 cups half and half and 2/3 cup (80 g) all-purpose flour until smooth.

    ### Step 3

    If using whole green chiles, split open, rinse seeds, and drain on paper towels.

    ### Step 4

    In a 9 x 13 casserole dish, assemble the layers:
    1. A layer of green chiles on the bottom.
    2. Half of the grated cheddar or jack cheese (about 8 oz).
    3. The remaining green chiles.
    4. The remaining cheese (leaving half of the total cheese, 8 oz, reserved for the top).

    ### Step 5

    Pour the beaten egg mixture evenly over the assembled layers of chiles and cheese. Sprinkle the reserved cheese on top of the casserole.

    ### Step 6

    Bake uncovered at 350°F, checking for doneness after 45 minutes. Cover with foil if the cheese begins to brown too quickly.

    ### Step 7

    Serve warm garnished with chunky salsa.
