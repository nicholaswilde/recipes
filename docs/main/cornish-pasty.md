# Cornish Pasty

![Cornish Pasty][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 4 | 3.33 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 500 g strong white bread flour
- 2 tsp salt
- 400 g vegetable shortening
- 150 ml ice-cold water
- 200 ml vegetable stock
- 0.75 tsp garlic powder
- 0.75 tsp onion granules
- 0.25 tsp dried rosemary
- 0.25 tsp dried thyme
- 1 Tbsp nutritional yeast
- 1.5 tsp soy sauce
- 20 g cornflour
- 3 Tbsp water
- 200 g potato (peeled and diced 1cm cubes)
- 100 g swede (rutabaga) (peeled and diced 1cm cubes)
- 75 g mushrooms (chopped)
- 75 g onion (peeled and diced)
- 2 tsp dairy-free margarine
- 2 Tbsp dairy-free milk

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 bowl
- 1 saucepan

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

### Step 2

Sift strong white bread flour and salt into a bowl. Rub vegetable shortening (chilled and cut into chunks) loosely into
the flour, leaving visible chunks. Add ice-cold water and mix briefly into a rough dough. Wrap and refrigerate for 1
hour.

### Step 3

On a floured surface, roll the dough into a 20x50cm rectangle. Fold the top third down and the bottom third up. Turn 90
degrees, roll out again, and repeat the fold. Refrigerate for 1.5 hours.

### Step 4

### Step 5

Combine vegetable stock, garlic powder, onion granules, dried rosemary, dried thyme, nutritional yeast, soy sauce,
cornflour, and water in a saucepan. Whisk constantly over heat until thickened and reduced. Remove from heat and let
cool completely until it reaches a jelly-like consistency.

### Step 6

### Step 7

Preheat oven to 190°C fan (210°C conventional). Roll the chilled pastry to 1cm thickness and cut into four 18cm
circles.

### Step 8

Place mixed potato (peeled and diced 1cm cubes), swede (rutabaga) (peeled and diced 1cm cubes), mushrooms (chopped), and
onion (peeled and diced) on one half of each circle.

### Step 9

Top with 2-3 teaspoons of cold gravy and dairy-free margarine per pasty.

### Step 10

Brush edges with dairy-free milk (for glaze), fold over, and crimp to seal. Cut three steam holes in the top and brush
with more milk.

### Step 11

Bake for 40 to 50 minutes until golden brown all over. Allow to cool slightly before serving as the filling will be very
hot.
