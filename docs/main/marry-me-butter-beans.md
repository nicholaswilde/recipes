# :butter: Marry Me Butter Beans

![Marry Me Butter Beans][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 4 | 7 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :butter: 2 Tbsp (28 g) unsalted butter
- :garlic: 4 cloves garlic
- :salt: 0.5 tsp salt
- :herb: 0.5 tsp oregano
- :hot_pepper: 0.5 tsp (1 g) crushed red pepper flakes
- :hot_pepper: 0.5 tsp smoked paprika
- :salt: 0.25 tsp (1 g) ground black pepper
- :tea: 0.5 cup (85 g) sun-dried tomatoes
- 1 cup [Vegetable Broth](../ingredients/vegetable-broth.md)
- :glass_of_milk: 2 cans butter beans
- :herb: 1 cup (42 g) fresh spinach
- :glass_of_milk: 1 cup (227 g) heavy cream
- :cheese_wedge: 0.5 cup (50 g) Parmesan
- :herb: 2 Tbsp (5 g) fresh basil

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 large saute pan

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Heat unsalted butter, garlic, salt, oregano, crushed red pepper flakes, smoked paprika, and ground black pepper in a
large saute pan over medium heat. Cook for about 2 minutes, until garlic is fragrant.

### Step 2

Stir in sun-dried tomatoes, [Vegetable Broth](../ingredients/vegetable-broth.md), and butter beans. Continue cooking on medium, uncovered, for 5 minutes.

### Step 3

Stir in fresh spinach until wilted, then stir in heavy cream, Parmesan, and fresh basil. Serve warm with crusty bread,
over rice, or with pasta.
