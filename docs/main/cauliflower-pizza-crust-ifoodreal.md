# :pizza: Cauliflower Pizza Crust iFoodReal

![Cauliflower Pizza Crust iFoodReal][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 50 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :broccoli: 2.5 lbs (1130 g) cauliflower
- :egg: 1 large egg (50 g)
- :cheese_wedge: 0.5 cup (50 g) Parmesan cheese (or Mozzarella cheese)
- :herb: 1 tsp (2 g) Italian seasoning
- :salt: 0.13 tsp (0.75 g) salt
- :salt: 0.25 tsp (0.5 g) black pepper

## :cooking: Cookware

- :cookie: 1 baking sheet
- :page_facing_up: 1 parchment paper
- :gear: 1 food processor
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 bowl
- :wastebasket: 1 cheesecloth
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 large bowl
- :spoon: 1 spatula
- :cookie: 1 pizza stone

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a **baking sheet** with unbleached **parchment paper**.

### Step 2

Rinse the **cauliflower**, remove outer leaves, and separate into florets. Process in a **food processor** in batches until a "rice" texture forms.

### Step 3

Spread the riced cauliflower on the prepared sheet pan and bake for 15 minutes.

### Step 4

Transfer the cooked cauliflower to a **bowl** lined with a double-layered **cheesecloth** (or linen towel). Let it cool for 10 to 15 minutes.

### Step 5

Once safe to touch, gather the corners of the cloth and squeeze the liquid out as hard as possible. You should aim to get about 1.5 cups of water out; the cauliflower must be very dry.

### Step 6

Increase the oven temperature to 450°F (230°C).

### Step 7

In a **large bowl**, combine the dry cauliflower, **egg**, **Parmesan cheese (or Mozzarella cheese)**, **Italian seasoning**, **salt**, and **black pepper**. Mix well with a **spatula**.

### Step 8

Line the baking pan (or a **pizza stone**) with fresh parchment paper and spray with cooking spray.

### Step 9

Place the dough in the center and flatten with your hands into an 11-inch diameter circle. Keep the edges from getting too thin to prevent burning.

### Step 10

Bake the crust for 20 minutes. For a crispier result, carefully flip the crust with a spatula and bake for a few additional minutes.

### Step 11

Add your desired pizza toppings and bake again until the cheese is golden brown and melted.

### Step 12

Let the pizza cool for 1 to 2 minutes before cutting into 8 slices.
