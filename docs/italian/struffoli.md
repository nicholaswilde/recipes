# Struffoli

![Struffoli][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 10 | 1.38 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :egg: 3 eggs (150 g)
- :candy: 1 tbsp granulated sugar
- :olive: 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- :salt: 1 pinch salt
- :tangerine: 1 orange
- :ear_of_rice: 2.5 cups (300 g) all purpose flour
- :olive: some light olive oil
- :honey_pot: 1 cup (336 g) honey
- :candy: 0.25 cup (50 g) granulated sugar
- :candy: some colored sprinkles

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 bowl
- 1 deep frying pan
- 1 baking sheet
- 1 slotted spoon
- 1 wide frying pan or skillet
- 1 serving plate

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

In a bowl, mix together eggs, granulated sugar, extra virgin olive oil and salt. Grate the orange (for zest) directly
into the bowl.

### Step 2

Slowly mix in the all purpose flour until a firm dough has formed. Turn out onto a floured surface and knead for 10
minutes or so.

### Step 3

Wrap in plastic and allow it to rest at room temperature for about 1 hour.

### Step 4

Cut the dough into 8 pieces and keep covered. Take one piece of dough and roll into a rope about 18 inches long. Cut
into small pieces about 0.5 inch lengths or slightly smaller than a marble. Repeat with all the dough.

### Step 5

Heat light olive oil in a deep frying pan filled with about 1 inch of oil to 375Â°F. Prepare a baking sheet lined with
paper towels.

### Step 6

When the oil is ready, fry handfuls of the struffoli, stirring to separate the pieces and allowing them to brown and
puff up. Remove from the oil with a slotted spoon and drain on the paper towel to remove excess oil. Repeat with all the
dough.

### Step 7

To make the honey sauce and coat the dough balls: Place the honey and granulated sugar in a wide frying pan or skillet.
Heat to dissolve the sugar then bring to a gentle boil. Simmer for 3 minutes to 5 minutes until the sauce is glossy and
thickens a little. Don't let it brown or overcook.

### Step 8

Add the fried dough balls, stir and cook over low-medium heat for 5 minutes until all the honey balls are coated.

### Step 9

Remove from the heat and allow to stand for a few minutes to cool and thicken. Stir well.

### Step 10

Pile on a serving plate and decorate with colored sprinkles.
