# :pie: Brioche Tart

![Brioche Tart][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8 to 10 | 12.21 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :tea: 1 pkg (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
- :glass_of_milk: 0.33 cup warm whole milk (100 to 110 degrees)
- :candy: 1 Tbsp sugar
- :ear_of_rice: 0.75 cup all-purpose flour
- :egg: 2 large eggs
- :ear_of_rice: 2 cups all-purpose flour
- :candy: 0.5 cup sugar
- :herb: 0.25 tsp ground mace
- :salt: 1 tsp salt
- :egg: 2 large eggs
- :butter: 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter
- :ear_of_rice: 0.5 cup all-purpose flour
- :bread: 0.5 recipe brioche dough
- :baby_bottle: 1 cup crème fraîche (or sour cream)
- :egg: 1 large egg
- :candy: 0.5 cup sugar
- :egg: 1 large egg white
- :candy: some crystal sugar
- :candy: some sugar
- :flower_playing_cards: 2 vanilla beans
- :droplet: 0.33 cup water
- :wine_glass: some dry white wine
- :egg: 4 large egg yolks
- :glass_of_milk: 1 cup heavy cream
- :apple: some dried fruits (raisins, prunes, apricots, peaches)
- :apple: some firm fresh fruits (apricots, peaches, nectarines, plums)
- :chestnut: some toasted chopped almonds
- :candy: some confectioner's sugar

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: stand mixer
- :spoon: wooden spoon
- :gear: paddle attachment
- :candy: plastic wrap
- :gear: dough hook
- :cookie: baking sheet
- :page_facing_up: parchment paper
- :shallow_pan_of_food: 10-inch flan ring
- :wastebasket: cooling rack
- :shallow_pan_of_food: heavy saucepan
- :paintbrush: pastry brush
- :wastebasket: strainer
- :cup_with_straw: heatproof measuring cup
- :hotsprings: saucepan of boiling water

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

### Step 2

Combine :tea: active dry yeast (7 g), :glass_of_milk: warm whole milk (100 to 110 degrees) (76 g), and :candy: sugar (12 g) in the bowl of a :gear: stand
mixer. Let stand 5 minutes for the yeast to soften. Stir in :ear_of_rice: all-purpose flour (90 g) with a :spoon: wooden spoon to make a firm,
sticky lump of dough.

### Step 3

Add :egg: large eggs (at room temperature) to the bowl and beat them in with the :gear: paddle attachment on medium speed until the
batter is smooth.

### Step 4

Scrape the bowl and beater, and sprinkle :ear_of_rice: all-purpose flour (240 g) over the batter. Do not mix the flour in. Cover the
bowl tightly with :candy: plastic wrap and set it aside at room temperature for about 3 hours, until the :tea: yeast mixture bubbles
up and almost completely engulfs the flour.

### Step 5

### Step 6

Add :candy: sugar (99 g), :herb: ground mace, :salt: salt (6 g), and :egg: large eggs (at room temperature) to the sponge.

### Step 7

Beat with the :gear: paddle attachment on low speed for about 1 minute just to combine all the ingredients. Increase the speed
to medium and beat for 1.5 minutes.

### Step 8

Add :butter: unsalted butter (227 g) (cut into 10 pieces, very soft) one piece at a time, beating on low speed until each piece
is incorporated. Scrape the bowl and beater, then beat for 2 minutes more on medium speed.

### Step 9

Attach the :gear: dough hook. Add :ear_of_rice: all-purpose flour (60 g) and knead it in thoroughly on low speed. Then knead on medium speed
for 2 to 3 minutes. Sprinkle the top of the dough with 2 to 3 tablespoons flour. Cover the bowl tightly with :candy: plastic
wrap.

### Step 10

Let rise at room temperature until tripled in volume, 5 to 6 hours.

### Step 11

Refrigerate the risen dough (don't deflate) for 1 to 2 hours.

### Step 12

Place the dough onto a lightly floured work surface. Gently pat the dough into a rectangle measuring about 8 by 12
inches. Fold the dough in thirds, like a business letter, enclose it tightly in :candy: plastic wrap, and refrigerate it
overnight.

### Step 13

### Step 14

Center rack in oven and preheat to 275°F. Line a :cookie: baking sheet with :page_facing_up: parchment paper and butter a :shallow_pan_of_food: 10-inch flan ring.

### Step 15

Work half of the chilled :bread: brioche dough into a ball, flatten to a 5-disk, and on a floured surface roll into 12-inch
circle. Center :shallow_pan_of_food: flan ring on dough and press to make a crimping guide. Place ring on lined :cookie: baking sheet. Crimp edge by
pinching dough between fingers, twisting slightly to make a diagonal crimp. Let dough fall about 1/4 inch inside
guideline. Lift dough up and into the ring. Working around the edge, firmly press down the base of the dough while
lifting the edge. Let rise, uncovered until doubled.

### Step 16

Whisk :baby_bottle: crème fraîche (or sour cream) (227 g) and :egg: large egg together. Press fingertips deeply into dough on bottom of
pan to make dimples. Spread :baby_bottle: crème fraîche mixture over bottom of tart. Sprinkle :candy: sugar (99 g) over :baby_bottle: crème fraîche.
Brush edge of tart with :egg: large egg white (beaten) and sprinkle with :candy: crystal sugar.

### Step 17

Bake 40 minutes, until crust is golden and custard is almost set. Transfer to a :wastebasket: cooling rack.

### Step 18

### Step 19

To make caramel syrup, put :candy: sugar (297 g) in a :shallow_pan_of_food: heavy saucepan. Split :flower_playing_cards: vanilla beans, scrape seeds into pan, and add pods.
Pour in :droplet: water (76 g) to cover, and bring to a boil. Wash down sides of pan with a :paintbrush: pastry brush dipped in cold water. As
soon as the :candy: sugar is a deep gold, immediately remove pan from heat and add :wine_glass: dry white wine (511 g). Return pan to heat
and bring syrup to a boil.

### Step 20

Pour 1 1/2 cups syrup through a :wastebasket: strainer into a :cup_with_straw: heatproof measuring cup. (Reserve remaining syrup for fruit garnish.)

### Step 21

Put :egg: large egg yolks into a mixer bowl and, whisking constantly, drizzle in hot syrup. Put bowl over a :hotsprings: saucepan of
boiling water and whisk constantly for 5 minutes. Attach the bowl to the mixer and whip on medium-low for 15 minutes,
until cool, pale, and tripled in volume. Fold in :glass_of_milk: heavy cream (227 g) (whipped to soft peaks).

### Step 22

### Step 23

Dice :apple: dried fruits (raisins, prunes, apricots, peaches) and soak in :droplet: hot water for several minutes, drain, and pat dry; or
slice firm :apple: fresh fruits (apricots, peaches, nectarines, plums).

### Step 24

Bring reserved caramel-wine syrup to a boil. Add :apple: fruit. Swirl pan and stir until fruit is softened.

### Step 25

To serve, place a slice of tart on each plate. Spoon on some sauce and caramel-poached :apple: fruit, and decorate with :chestnut: toasted
chopped almonds and a dusting of :candy: confectioner's sugar.
