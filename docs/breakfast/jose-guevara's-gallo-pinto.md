# :stuffed_flatbread: Jose Guevara's Gallo Pinto

![Jose Guevara's Gallo Pinto](../assets/images/jose-guevara's-gallo-pinto.png){ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 4 | 15 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :olive: 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
- :onion: 0.5 chopped onion
- :garlic: 1 clove garlic, minced
- :ear_of_rice: 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old and refrigerated)
- :takeout_box: 2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- :beans: 1 8-oz can cooked [black beans][1]
- :hot_pepper: some Salsa Lizano (optional)
- :herb: some cilantro

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 large skillet

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Sauté the onion and garlic until softened.

### Step 2

Add the rice and sauté for a few minutes, stirring frequently.

### Step 3

Add Worcestershire sauce and cooked [black beans][1]; turn heat to low and stir. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes more.

### Step 4

Garnish with cilantro and serve with Lizano sauce if desired.
