# :pancakes: Blueberry Smoothie Pancakes

![Blueberry Smoothie Pancakes][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 30 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :apple: 1.5 cups (234 g) blueberries
- :apple: 1 banana
- :glass_of_milk: 0.75 cup (170 g) milk
- :egg: 1 egg
- :bread: 1.75 cups (198 g) whole wheat flour
- :chestnut: 2 tsp baking powder
- :salt: 0.25 tsp salt
- :leafy_green: some yogurt (optional)
- :chestnut: some cinnamon (optional)
- :leafy_green: some nuts (optional)
- :leafy_green: some honey (optional)

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: 1 blender
- 1 pan

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Blitz half the blueberries, banana, milk, egg, and whole wheat flour with baking powder and salt in a blender. Fold in
remaining blueberries.

### Step 2

Place oil in heated pan, then pour batter to make pancakes. Top with yogurt (optional), cinnamon (optional), nuts
(optional), and honey (optional).
