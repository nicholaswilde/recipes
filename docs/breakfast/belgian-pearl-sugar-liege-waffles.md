# :candy: Belgian Pearl Sugar Liege Waffles

![Belgian Pearl Sugar Liege Waffles][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 12 | 1.30 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :tea: 7 g rapid-rise yeast
- :droplet: 0.75 cup (170 g) warm milk
- :bread: 440 g all-purpose flour
- :maple_leaf: 50 g brown sugar
- :salt: 8 g salt
- :egg: 2 large eggs
- :flower_playing_cards: 2 tsp vanilla extract
- :butter: 226 g butter
- :chestnut: some cinnamon
- :candy: 226 g Lars Own Belgian Pearl Sugar

## :cooking: Cookware

- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 small bowl
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 large mixing bowl
- 1 greased cookie sheet
- 1 greased waffle iron

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Dissolve rapid-rise yeast (1 packet) in warm milk (100°F) in a small bowl and set aside until bubbly.

### Step 2

Combine the yeast mixture, all-purpose flour, brown sugar, salt, large eggs, vanilla extract, and softened butter
(optionally adding cinnamon) in a large mixing bowl; mix until well blended and a soft dough forms.

### Step 3

Cover and let rise until doubled in size, 30 to 60 minutes.

### Step 4

Stir in Lars Own Belgian Pearl Sugar until evenly distributed.

### Step 5

Divide dough into 12 equal portions (3.5 ounces each); form into patties and place on a greased cookie sheet.

### Step 6

Cover and allow to rise 15 minutes.

### Step 7

Bake in a preheated and greased waffle iron for 2 to 3 minutes or until deep golden brown.
