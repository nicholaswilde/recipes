# :ear_of_rice: Golden Milk Oatmeal

![Golden Milk Oatmeal][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 17 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :glass_of_milk: 2 cups plant-based milk
- :chestnut: 4 Medjool dates
- :ear_of_rice: 0.75 cup rolled oats
- :ear_of_rice: 2 tbsp teff
- :sweet_potato: 2 tsp fresh ginger
- :curry: 1 tsp ground turmeric
- :chestnut: 0.25 tsp ground cloves
- :sweet_potato: 0.25 tsp ground ginger
- :chestnut: 0.5 tsp ground cinnamon
- :salt: 0.13 tsp black pepper
- :apple: some sliced figs
- :seedling: some hemp seeds

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: blender
- :shallow_pan_of_food: medium saucepan
- :spoon: spatula

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Combine :glass_of_milk: plant-based milk and pitted :chestnut: Medjool dates in a :gear: blender and process until smooth.

### Step 2

In a :shallow_pan_of_food: medium saucepan, toast :ear_of_rice: rolled oats and :ear_of_rice: teff over medium heat for 2 minutes.

### Step 3

Add the blended milk mixture, grated :sweet_potato: fresh ginger, :curry: ground turmeric, :chestnut: ground cloves, :sweet_potato: ground ginger, :chestnut: ground cinnamon, and
:salt: black pepper to the saucepan and stir well with a :spoon: spatula.

### Step 4

Bring the mixture to a low boil, stir, and cover.

### Step 5

Reduce heat to medium-low and cook for 15 minutes.

### Step 6

Top with :apple: sliced figs and :seedling: hemp seeds, if desired.
