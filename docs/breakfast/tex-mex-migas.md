# :tea: Tex-Mex Migas

![Tex-Mex Migas][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 2 | 0 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :tea: 0.5 onion
- :hot_pepper: 1 jalapeño or serrano pepper
- :butter: 2 tbsp butter
- :egg: 6 large eggs
- :salt: some salt
- :salt: some black pepper
- :bread: 1 cup (170 g) tortilla chips
- :tomato: 1 roma tomato
- :cheese_wedge: 0.5 cup (56 g) Monterey Jack cheese
- :herb: some cilantro
- :tomato: some salsa

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 pan
- 1 bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Sauté finely chopped onion and finely chopped jalapeño or serrano pepper in a pan with butter (or oil) until softened.

### Step 2

Whisk large eggs with a pinch of salt and black pepper in a bowl.

### Step 3

Add the egg mixture to the pan and cook over medium-low heat, stirring gently.

### Step 4

When the eggs are about half cooked, fold in crumbled tortilla chips and seeded and diced roma tomato.

### Step 5

Stir in shredded Monterey Jack cheese until melted.

### Step 6

Top with fresh cilantro and serve immediately with your favorite salsa.
