# :muffin: Pumpkin Cream Cheese Muffins

![Pumpkin Cream Cheese Muffins][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 32 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :cheese_wedge: 8 oz cream cheese
- :candy: 0.25 cup granulated sugar
- :egg: 1 egg yolk
- :flower_playing_cards: 0.5 tsp vanilla extract
- :bread: 3 cups all-purpose flour
- :candy: 3.4 oz instant vanilla pudding mix
- :chestnut: 2 tsp baking soda
- :salt: 1 tsp salt
- :chestnut: 1.5 tsp ground cinnamon
- :chestnut: 0.5 tsp ground nutmeg
- :melon: 15 oz pumpkin puree
- :candy: 1 cup granulated sugar
- :candy: 1 cup brown sugar
- :egg: 5 eggs
- :olive: 0.5 cup vegetable oil
- :butter: 0.5 cup unsalted butter
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- :candy: 2 tbsp cinnamon sugar
- :seedling: some pepitas

## :cooking: Cookware

- :cookie: muffin tin
- :bowl_with_spoon: small bowl
- :bowl_with_spoon: large bowl
- :bowl_with_spoon: medium bowl
- :spoon: spatula
- :spoon: small spoon
- :package: piping bag
- :knife: toothpick
- :wastebasket: wire rack

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a :cookie: muffin tin with paper liners.

### Step 2

In a :bowl_with_spoon: small bowl, beat softened :cheese_wedge: cream cheese, :candy: granulated sugar, :egg: egg yolk, and :flower_playing_cards: vanilla extract until smooth. Set aside.

### Step 3

In a :bowl_with_spoon: large bowl, whisk together :bread: all-purpose flour, :candy: instant vanilla pudding mix, :chestnut: baking soda, :salt: salt, :chestnut: ground cinnamon, and
:chestnut: ground nutmeg.

### Step 4

In a :bowl_with_spoon: medium bowl, whisk :melon: pumpkin puree, :candy: granulated sugar, :candy: brown sugar, :egg: eggs, :olive: vegetable oil, melted :butter: unsalted butter, and
:flower_playing_cards: vanilla extract until well combined.

### Step 5

Fold the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients with a :spoon: spatula until just combined.

### Step 6

Fill each muffin cup about 3/4 full with the pumpkin batter.

### Step 7

Dollop 1 tbsp of the cream cheese mixture into the center of each muffin using a :spoon: small spoon or :package: piping bag.

### Step 8

Sprinkle with :candy: cinnamon sugar and :seedling: pepitas.

### Step 9

Bake for 22.5 minutes until a :knife: toothpick inserted into the pumpkin part comes out clean.

### Step 10

Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a :wastebasket: wire rack.
