# Vegan Tourtiere

![Vegan Tourtiere][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8 | 1 hour 45 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :cheese_wedge: 2 Tbsp (9 g) nutritional yeast
- :butter: 1 Tbsp (14 g) vegan butter
- :takeout_box: 1 Tbsp (18 g) soy sauce
- :cheese_wedge: 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu
- :butter: 1 Tbsp (14 g) vegan butter
- :onion: 1 small (150 g) yellow onion
- :garlic: 3 cloves garlic
- :mushroom: 16 oz (454 g) button mushrooms
- :stew: 0.75 cup (170 g) vegetable broth
- :chestnut: 3 Tbsp (24 g) cornstarch
- :bread: 0.5 cup (54 g) breadcrumbs
- :salt: 0.75 tsp (4 g) salt
- :hot_pepper: 0.5 tsp (1 g) smoked paprika
- :herb: 0.5 tsp (1 g) dried thyme
- :salt: 0.25 tsp (1 g) black pepper
- :herb: 0.25 tsp (1 g) dried sage
- :chestnut: 0.13 tsp (0.5 g) ground cloves
- :bread: 1 pkg (approx. 400 g) double pie crust
- :baby_bottle: some aquafaba
- :glass_of_milk: some plant milk

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 baking sheet
- 1 parchment paper
- 1 large bowl
- 1 large skillet
- 1 small jar
- 1 9-inch pie plate

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

### Step 2

In a large bowl, whisk together 2 Tbsp (9 g) nutritional yeast, 1 Tbsp (14 g) vegan butter (melted), and 1 Tbsp (18 g) soy
sauce. Crumble 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu into the bowl using your fingers until it looks like ground meat. Toss
well to coat evenly.

### Step 3

Spread tofu on the baking sheet and bake until browned and slightly chewy, 35 to 40 minutes, stirring occasionally. Set
aside.

### Step 4

Increase oven temperature to 400°F.

### Step 5

In a large skillet over medium heat, melt 1 Tbsp (14 g) vegan butter. Add 1 small (150 g) yellow onion (chopped) and
sauté until translucent, about 5 minutes.

### Step 6

Add 3 cloves garlic (minced) and 16 oz (454 g) button mushrooms (finely chopped). Cook until mushrooms have released
their liquid and it has mostly evaporated, 8 to 10 minutes.

### Step 7

In a small jar or bowl, whisk 0.75 cup (170 g) vegetable broth with 3 Tbsp (24 g) cornstarch until smooth.

### Step 8

Pour broth mixture into the skillet. Add baked tofu crumbles, 0.5 cup (54 g) breadcrumbs, 0.75 tsp (4 g) salt, 0.5 tsp
(1 g) smoked paprika, 0.5 tsp (1 g) dried thyme, 0.25 tsp (1 g) black pepper, 0.25 tsp (1 g) dried sage, and 0.13 tsp
(0.5 g) ground cloves.

### Step 9

Stir until mixture thickens. If it looks too dry, add more vegetable broth (up to 0.25 cups). Remove from heat.

### Step 10

Roll out half of double pie crust and line a 9-inch pie plate. Spoon filling into the crust and pack it down firmly.

### Step 11

Roll out the second half of the dough and place it on top. Trim, crimp the edges, and cut a few slits in the top to vent
steam. Brush top of crust with aquafaba or plant milk.

### Step 12

Bake until crust is golden brown, about 40 minutes.

### Step 13

Let pie rest for at least 10 minutes before slicing to allow filling to set.
