# :takeout_box: Teriyaki Sauce

![Teriyaki Sauce](../../../assets/images/teriyaki-sauce.jpg){ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1.25 cups | 10 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :takeout_box: 0.5 cup (120 g) tamari
- :candy: 0.25 cup (53 g) brown sugar
- :sweet_potato: 1.5 tsp fresh ginger (minced)
- :garlic: 1 tsp garlic (minced)
- :honey_pot: 1 Tbsp (21 g) honey
- :olive: 1 tsp sesame oil
- :wine_glass: 3 Tbsp mirin
- :droplet: 0.25 cup (57 g) water
- :chestnut: 3 tsp (7 g) cornstarch

## :cooking: Cookware

- :shallow_pan_of_food: 1 small saucepan
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Combine :takeout_box: tamari, :candy: brown sugar, minced :sweet_potato: fresh ginger, minced :garlic: garlic, :honey_pot: honey, :olive: sesame oil, and :wine_glass: mirin in a small saucepan.

### Step 2

In a bowl, mix :droplet: water with :chestnut: cornstarch.

### Step 3

Add the cornstarch mixture to the saucepan.

### Step 4

Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 4 minutes.

### Step 5

Remove from heat and let cool.

!!! tip
    If using as a marinade, omit the cornstarch and let the sauce cool completely before using.
