# :apple: Indian Mango Chutney

![Indian Mango Chutney][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 2.5 pints | 1.03 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :oil_drum: 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
- :sweet_potato: 1 Tbsp fresh ginger
- :garlic: 2 cloves garlic
- :hot_pepper: 1 red chili
- :seedling: 0.5 tsp nigella seeds
- :chestnut: 0.5 tsp cumin seeds
- :chestnut: 0.5 tsp ground coriander
- :curry: 0.5 tsp ground turmeric
- :chestnut: 0.25 tsp ground cloves
- :chestnut: 0.25 tsp ground cardamom
- :chestnut: 0.25 tsp ground cinnamon
- :apple: 5 large mangoes
- :candy: 2 cups granulated sugar
- :salt: 0.25 tsp salt
- :wine_glass: 1 cup white vinegar

## :cooking: Cookware

- :shallow_pan_of_food: medium stock pot
- :spoon: potato masher

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Heat the :oil_drum: vegetable oil over medium-high in a :shallow_pan_of_food: medium stock pot. Saute the :sweet_potato: fresh ginger (finely minced), :garlic: garlic (finely
minced), and :hot_pepper: red chili (finely minced) for 1 minute.

### Step 2

Add the :seedling: nigella seeds, :chestnut: cumin seeds, :chestnut: ground coriander, :curry: ground turmeric, :chestnut: ground cloves, :chestnut: ground cardamom, and :chestnut: ground
cinnamon and saute for another 1 minute.

### Step 3

Add the diced :apple: mangoes (about 1500 g), :candy: granulated sugar (400 g), :salt: salt, and :wine_glass: white vinegar (240 ml) and stir to combine.
Bring it to a rapid boil and reduce to medium-low.

### Step 4

Steady simmer for 1 hour.

### Step 5

Remove from heat and allow it to cool. If you prefer smaller chunks, use a :spoon: potato masher to mash the mixture to the
desired consistency.

### Step 6

For storage, chutney will keep for a while because of the high acidity content. Up to 2 months in the fridge (in a
sealed jar) or for several months in the freezer. For best results let the chutney sit for a few days before using to
allow the flavors to develop and mellow.
