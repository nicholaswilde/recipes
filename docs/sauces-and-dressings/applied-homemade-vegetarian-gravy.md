# :stew: Applied Homemade Vegetarian Gravy

![Applied Homemade Vegetarian Gravy][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 6 cups | 30 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :butter: 1 stick unsalted butter
- :onion: 1 cup onion
- :leafy_green: 0.5 cups celery
- :carrot: 0.5 cup carrot
- :garlic: 3 cloves garlic
- :ear_of_rice: 0.75 cup all-purpose flour
- :stew: 6 cups vegetable broth
- :salt: some salt
- :salt: some pepper

## :cooking: Cookware

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Heat a saute pan over medium heat.

### Step 2

Add unsalted butter, minced onion, minced celery, minced carrot, and sliced garlic to the pan.

### Step 3

Cook gently (sweat) for 2 minutes until the vegetables soften.

### Step 4

Stir in the all-purpose flour and cook for an additional 4 minutes, or until the roux turns a light brown color.

### Step 5

Gradually add the vegetable broth while stirring to avoid lumps.

### Step 6

Simmer the gravy for 5 minutes, allowing it to thicken.

### Step 7

Season with salt and pepper to taste.

### Step 8

If desired, finish by stirring in about 1 Tbsp of your favorite combination of freshly chopped herbs.

### Step 9

Storage: keeps well in the refrigerator for up to 5 days or can be frozen for 2-3 months.

### Step 10

Pro-tip: To make this gravy vegan, substitute the butter with oil or a plant-based butter alternative.
