# :green_salad: Broccoli Salad Dressing

![Broccoli Salad Dressing][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 6 | 60 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 1 cup [mayonnaise][2]
- :wine_glass: 2 Tbsp (25 g) red or white wine vinegar
- :candy: 0.5 cup (78 g) sugar
- :salt: 0.5 tsp salt

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Mix mayonnaise, red or white wine vinegar, sugar, and salt.

### Step 2

Chill at least 1 hour before tossing with salad.
