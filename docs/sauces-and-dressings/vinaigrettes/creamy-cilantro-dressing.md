# :herb: Creamy Cilantro Dressing

![Creamy Cilantro Dressing][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8 | 5 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :cheese_wedge: 12 oz organic firm silken tofu
- :herb: 1 bunch cilantro
- :garlic: 2 cloves garlic
- :tea: 0.5 white onion
- :apple: 3 Tbsp (52 g) apple cider vinegar
- :droplet: 0.25 cup (57 g) water
- :tangerine: 0.5 tsp (2 g) lemon juice
- :salt: some salt

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 blender

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

In a blender, organic firm silken tofu, cilantro, garlic, white onion, apple cider vinegar, water, lemon juice, and salt
until well combined.

### Step 2

Serve with your favorite salad, power bowl, steamed veggies, or wrap.
