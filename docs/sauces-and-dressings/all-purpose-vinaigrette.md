# All-Purpose Vinaigrette

![All-Purpose Vinaigrette][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 3/4 cup | 0 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :grapes: 0.5 cup balsamic vinegar
- :hotdog: 0.5 cup Dijon mustard
- :garlic: 0.25 cup minced shallot
- :salt: some ground black pepper

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 small bowl
- 1 airtight container

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Combine balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced shallot, and ground black pepper in a small bowl and mix well. Store
refrigerated in an airtight container for up to a week.
