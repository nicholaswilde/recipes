# :beans: Edamame Hummus

![Edamame Hummus][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 4 | 10 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :beans: 1.5 cups (233 g) shelled edamame (steamed or boiled)
- :takeout_box: 1 Tbsp tahini
- :tangerine: 2 lemons lemon juice
- :garlic: 2 garlic cloves (minced)
- :chestnut: 1 tsp ground cumin
- :droplet: 0.25 cup (57 g) water
- :herb: 0.25 cup fresh cilantro (finely chopped)
- :salt: some salt
- :salt: some pepper

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 blender

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

In a blender, process shelled edamame, tahini, lemon juice, garlic cloves, ground cumin, water, fresh cilantro, and salt
and pepper to taste until smooth. Add additional water if needed for a smooth consistency.
