# :carrot: Healthy Carrot Cookies

![Healthy Carrot Cookies](../assets/images/healthy-carrot-cookies.jpg){ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 12 cookies | 30 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :ear_of_rice: 0.75 cup (72 g) [almond flour][1]
- :chestnut: 0.5 tsp baking soda
- :custard: 1.5 tsp cinnamon
- :chestnut: 0.13 tsp nutmeg
- :salt: 0.25 tsp salt
- :honey_pot: 0.25 cup (85 g) maple syrup
- :baby_bottle: 2 Tbsp (32 g) almond butter
- :icecream: 1 tsp vanilla
- :carrot: 1 cup (110 g) grated carrots
- :ear_of_rice: 0.5 cup (45 g) rolled oats
- :chestnut: 0.25 cup (30 g) chopped walnuts
- :grapes: 0.25 cup (40 g) raisins

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 medium bowl
- 1 small bowl
- 1 baking sheet
- 1 parchment paper

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

### Step 2

In a medium bowl, mix [almond flour][1], baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.

### Step 3

In a small bowl, whisk together maple syrup, almond butter, and vanilla.

### Step 4

Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until well combined.

### Step 5

Fold in the grated carrots, rolled oats, walnuts, and raisins.

### Step 6

Scoop about 2 tablespoons of dough for each cookie onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten slightly with your hand.

### Step 7

Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until the edges are lightly browned.

### Step 8

Allow to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
