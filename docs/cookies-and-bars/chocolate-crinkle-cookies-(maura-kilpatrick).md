# :cookie: Chocolate Crinkle Cookies (Maura Kilpatrick)

![Chocolate Crinkle Cookies (Maura Kilpatrick)][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 24 cookies | 1.00 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :bread: 1.25 cups (150 g) all-purpose flour
- :candy: 0.5 cup (42 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
- :chestnut: 2 tsp (8 g) baking powder
- :salt: 0.5 tsp (3 g) fleur de sel or kosher salt
- :butter: 0.5 cup (113 g) unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
- :candy: 1.5 cups (320 g) light brown sugar
- :egg: 2 large (100 g) eggs
- :chestnut: 8 ounces (227 g) extra-bittersweet chocolate (70% cacao, melted and cooled)
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp vanilla extract
- :glass_of_milk: 0.33 cup (75 g) whole milk
- :page_facing_up: some parchment paper
- :candy: 1.5 cups (170 g) confectioners' sugar
- :candy: 1.5 cups (297 g) granulated sugar

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: 1 stand mixer
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 bowl
- :cookie: 1 baking sheet
- :cookie: 1 2-tablespoon cookie scoop

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Sift together :bread: all-purpose flour, :candy: unsweetened cocoa powder, :chestnut: baking powder, and :salt: fleur de sel or kosher salt; set aside.

### Step 2

Combine the :butter: unsalted butter (chilled and cubed) and :candy: light brown sugar in the bowl of a :gear: stand mixer fitted with a paddle
attachment. Mix on medium speed until light and very fluffy (5 minutes). Scrape the bowl.

### Step 3

Lower the speed and add the :egg: large eggs one at a time, scraping between additions.

### Step 4

Still on low speed, add the :chestnut: extra-bittersweet chocolate (70% cacao, melted and cooled). Mix until well combined (1
minute). Add the :flower_playing_cards: vanilla extract.

### Step 5

Staying on low speed, alternate adding the flour mixture and the :glass_of_milk: whole milk. Begin and end with the flour mixture in
three additions, scraping the bowl between additions.

### Step 6

Transfer the dough (which will be like a wet batter) to a :bowl_with_spoon: bowl; cover and refrigerate at least overnight (up to 1 week).

### Step 7

Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a :cookie: baking sheet with :page_facing_up: parchment paper. Put :candy: confectioners' sugar and :candy: granulated sugar in
separate bowls.

### Step 8

Use a :cookie: 2-tablespoon cookie scoop to shape the dough into balls. Roll each ball in the :candy: granulated sugar first, then in the
:candy: powdered sugar, coating completely.

### Step 9

Place cookies on the :cookie: baking sheet 0.5-inch apart. Bake until puffed and cracked and just set around the edges, 14 to 16
minutes (centers will still be soft).

### Step 10

Cool completely on the :cookie: baking sheet.
