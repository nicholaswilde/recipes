# :ear_of_rice: Chewy Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Kroll's Korner

![Chewy Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies Kroll's Korner][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 1 hour |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :bread: 1 cup (120 g) all-purpose flour
- :bread: 0.75 cup (90 g) cake flour
- :ear_of_rice: 2 cups (198 g) old fashioned oats
- :chestnut: 1 tsp (3 g) cornstarch
- :chestnut: 0.25 tsp (1 g) ground cinnamon (optional)
- :chestnut: 0.25 tsp (1 g) ground nutmeg (optional)
- :chestnut: 0.5 tsp (3 g) baking soda
- :chestnut: 0.5 tsp (2 g) baking powder
- :salt: 0.5 tsp (3 g) salt
- :butter: 0.5 cup (113 g) unsalted butter
- :candy: 0.5 cup (107 g) brown sugar
- :candy: 0.25 cup (50 g) granulated sugar
- :egg: 1 large egg (50 g)
- :egg: 1 large egg yolk (14 g)
- :candy: 1 Tbsp (21 g) molasses
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp (4 g) pure vanilla extract
- :chocolate_bar: 2 cups (340 g) milk chocolate chips
- :candy: 1 cup (113 g) confectioners sugar
- :glass_of_milk: 1.5 Tbsp (21 g) whole milk
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp (4 g) pure vanilla extract

## :cooking: Cookware

- :cookie: 1 baking sheet
- :page_facing_up: 1 parchment paper
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 large bowl
- :gear: 1 stand mixer
- :snowflake: 1 fridge
- :wastebasket: 1 cooling rack
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 medium bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat the oven to 400°F. Line a **baking sheet** with **parchment paper** or a silicone baking mat.

### Step 2

In a **large bowl**, whisk together **all-purpose flour** (120 g), **cake flour** (90 g), **old fashioned oats** (198 g), **cornstarch** (3 g), **ground cinnamon** (optional), **ground nutmeg** (optional), **baking soda** (3 g), **baking powder** (2 g), and **salt** (3 g). Set aside.

### Step 3

Place cold, cubed **unsalted butter** (113 g) into the bowl of a **stand mixer** with the paddle attachment. Turn the mixer on the lowest setting and cream the butter for about 30 seconds.

### Step 4

Add **brown sugar** (107 g) and mix for 30 seconds, followed by **granulated sugar** (50 g) until incorporated.

### Step 5

Add **egg**, **egg yolk**, **molasses** (21 g), and **pure vanilla extract** (4 g). Blend until mixed, scraping down the sides of the bowl as needed.

### Step 6

Keep the mixer on low speed and gradually add the dry ingredients (about 1/4 cup at a time).

### Step 7

Mix in **milk chocolate chips** (340 g) until incorporated, then turn off the machine. (If the dough is sticky, chill it in the **fridge** for a few minutes).

### Step 8

Measure out about 6 to 7 large cookies (or smaller if preferred). Roll each into a ball, place on the prepared cookie sheet, and gently press down slightly to flatten.

### Step 9

Bake for 9 to 11 minutes. Pull them out before they look fully done. Let them rest on the cookie sheet for 15 minutes before transferring to a **cooling rack**.

### Step 10

Whisk **confectioners sugar** (113 g), **whole milk** (21 g), and **pure vanilla extract** (4 g) together in a **medium bowl** and drizzle over the cooled cookies.
