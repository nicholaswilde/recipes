# :cookie: Weight Watchers Double Oatmeal Cookies

![Weight Watchers Double Oatmeal Cookies][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 14 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

=== "full batch"

    - :ear_of_rice: 1.5 cups (180 g) all-purpose flour
    - :ear_of_rice: 3 cups (297 g) quick oats
    - :cup_with_straw: 1 tsp baking soda
    - :salt: 0.5 tsp salt
    - :custard: 2 tsp cinnamon
    - :sweet_potato: 1 tsp ginger
    - :four_leaf_clover: 0.5 tsp cloves
    - :butter: 1 cup (226 g) unsalted butter
    - :maple_leaf: 1.5 cups (320 g) light brown sugar
    - :candy: 0.5 cup (99 g) granulated sugar
    - :egg: 2 egg whites
    - :chocolate_bar: 0.25 cup (44 g) mini chocolate chips

=== "half batch"

    - :ear_of_rice: 0.75 cups (90 g) all-purpose flour
    - :ear_of_rice: 1.5 cups (149 g) quick oats
    - :cup_with_straw: 0.5 tsp baking soda
    - :salt: 0.25 tsp salt
    - :custard: 1 tsp cinnamon
    - :sweet_potato: 0.5 tsp ginger
    - :four_leaf_clover: 0.25 tsp cloves
    - :butter: 0.5 cup (113 g) unsalted butter
    - :maple_leaf: 0.75 cups (160 g) light brown sugar
    - :candy: 0.25 cup (49 g) granulated sugar
    - :egg: 1 egg white
    - :chocolate_bar: 0.125 cup (22 g) mini chocolate chips

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 large bowl
- 1 stand mixer

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 350Â°F.

### Step 2

Whisk the all-purpose flour, quick oats, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger, and cloves in a large bowl.

### Step 3

Cream room temperature unsalted butter, light brown sugar, and granulated sugar for a few minutes in a stand mixer.

### Step 4

Add the egg whites to the mixer and mix for a few minutes.

### Step 5

Slowly fold in the dry ingredients into the batter.

### Step 6

Fold in the mini chocolate chips.

### Step 7

Chill for 24 hours. Bake for 11 to 14 minutes.
