# :cookie: Thumbprint Cookies

![Thumbprint Cookies][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 24 cookies | 1.00 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :butter: 1 cup (227 g) unsalted butter, softened
- :candy: 0.33 cup (66 g) granulated sugar
- :candy: 0.33 cup (71 g) light brown sugar
- :egg: 1 large (20 g) egg yolk
- :flower_playing_cards: 0.75 tsp vanilla extract
- :bread: 2.25 cups (270 g) all-purpose flour
- :chestnut: 2 tsp (5 g) cornstarch
- :salt: 0.5 tsp salt
- :candy: 0.5 cup (99 g) granulated sugar
- :apple: 0.33 cup (113 g) jam or preserves

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: 1 stand mixer
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 small bowl
- :spoon: 1 tsp
- :snowflake: 1 freezer
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 microwave-safe bowl
- :cookie: 1 parchment-lined baking sheet
- :cookie: 1 baking sheet

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

In the bowl of a :gear: stand mixer, beat :butter: unsalted butter (softened) until creamy.

### Step 2

Scrape down the sides, add :candy: granulated sugar and :candy: light brown sugar, and beat until well-combined (about 30 to 60
seconds).

### Step 3

Add :egg: egg yolk and :flower_playing_cards: vanilla extract and beat well.

### Step 4

In a :bowl_with_spoon: small bowl, whisk together :bread: all-purpose flour, :chestnut: cornstarch, and :salt: salt.

### Step 5

With mixer on low speed, gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients until completely combined.

### Step 6

Scoop dough into :candy: granulated sugar (optional) and roll into 1 Tbsp sized balls until very smooth. Gently press an indent
in the center of each ball with your thumb or the back of a :spoon: tsp.

### Step 7

Transfer to the :snowflake: freezer and chill for 30 minutes.

### Step 8

Preheat oven to 375°F.

### Step 9

Heat :apple: jam or preserves in a :bowl_with_spoon: microwave-safe bowl for 5 to 10 seconds until fluid. Spoon jam into each thumbprint, filling
to the brim.

### Step 10

Place cookies 2" apart on a :cookie: parchment-lined baking sheet.

### Step 11

Bake for 11 minutes or until edges just begin to turn golden brown.

### Step 12

Allow cookies to cool completely on the :cookie: baking sheet before serving.
