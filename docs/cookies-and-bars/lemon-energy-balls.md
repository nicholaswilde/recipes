# Lemon Energy Balls

![Lemon Energy Balls][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 16 | 20 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :ear_of_rice: 1 cup (99 g) rolled oats
- :grapes: 1 heaping cup (198 g) Medjool dates
- :chestnut: 0.75 cup (72 g) almond flour
- :coconut: 0.5 cup (42 g) shredded coconut
- :tangerine: 2 Tbsp (30 g) lemon juice
- :tangerine: 1 Tbsp (6 g) lemon zest
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp (5 g) vanilla extract
- :salt: 0.25 tsp (2 g) salt
- :tangerine: 1 tsp (5 g) lemon juice
- :droplet: 1 tsp (5 g) water
- :coconut: some shredded coconut

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: 1 food processor

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Add rolled oats, pitted Medjool dates, almond flour, shredded coconut (unsweetened), lemon juice, lemon zest (about 2
lemons), vanilla extract (plus optional 0.125 tsp almond extract), and salt to a food processor. Blend until the mixture
sticks together when pressed between your fingers. Stop and scrape down the sides as needed. If the mixture looks too
dry, add lemon juice or water at a time until it comes together.

### Step 2

Scoop about 1 tablespoon of mixture and roll between your hands to form balls.

### Step 3

Roll the balls in additional shredded coconut to coat.

### Step 4

Enjoy right away or chill for 20 minutes for the best texture. Store in the fridge or freeze.

!!! tip

    For more flavor, toast the oats first! See [Toasted Rolled Oats](../ingredients/toasted-rolled-oats.md) for instructions.
