# Key Lime Pie Energy Balls

![Key Lime Pie Energy Balls][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 16 | 22 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :grapes: 1 cup (149 g) Medjool dates
- :ear_of_rice: 0.75 cup (74 g) rolled oats
- :chestnut: 0.5 cup (56 g) cashews
- :coconut: 0.25 cup (21 g) shredded coconut
- :bread: 3 Tbsp (19 g) graham cracker crumbs
- :tangerine: 2 limes
- :tangerine: 2 Tbsp (28 g) lime juice
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp (5 g) vanilla extract
- :coconut: some shredded coconut

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: 1 food processor

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Add pitted Medjool dates, rolled oats (old-fashioned), cashews, unsweetened shredded coconut, graham cracker crumbs, the
grated zest of limes, fresh lime juice, and vanilla extract to a food processor. Blend until the mixture looks sticky
and holds together when pressed between your fingers, about 1 to 2 minutes. If the mixture feels too dry, add 1 more
date or a small splash of lime juice.

### Step 2

Scoop about 1 tablespoon of mixture at a time and roll into balls.

### Step 3

Roll the balls in additional shredded coconut mixed with extra lime zest so the zest sticks to the outside.

### Step 4

Place them on a plate or tray and chill in the fridge for about 20 minutes until slightly firm.
