# :butter: Holiday Butter Cookies King Arthur Baking

![Holiday Butter Cookies King Arthur Baking][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 44 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :butter: 18 Tbsp (255 g) salted butter
- :candy: 1.25 cups (142 g) confectioners' sugar
- :egg: 1 large egg yolk
- :salt: 0.5 tsp table salt
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp vanilla extract
- :bread: 2.75 cups (330 g) all-purpose flour

## :cooking: Cookware

- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 large bowl
- :package: 1 plastic wrap
- :wood: 1 lightly floured surface
- :cookie: 1 cookie cutters
- :cookie: 1 parchment-lined baking sheets
- :wastebasket: 1 wire rack

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350°F.

### Step 2

In a large bowl, beat **salted butter** (255 g) (room temperature) and **confectioners' sugar** (142 g) until smooth.

### Step 3

Add **egg yolk**, **table salt**, and **vanilla extract** (or almond extract, or **Fiori di Sicilia**). Beat until smooth.

### Step 4

Add **all-purpose flour** (330 g) and mix until smooth. The mixture will seem dry at first but will suddenly come together.

### Step 5

Divide the dough in half, shape each half into a flattened disk, and wrap in **plastic wrap**. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours (or overnight).

### Step 6

Remove the dough from the refrigerator and let it soften for 20 to 30 minutes until it's pliable enough to roll.

### Step 7

On a **lightly floured surface**, roll the dough 1/8" to 3/16" thick. Cut into shapes with **cookie cutters**.

### Step 8

Place the cookies on ungreased or **parchment-lined baking sheets**. They can be close together as they barely spread.

### Step 9

Bake the cookies for 12 to 14 minutes until they're set and barely browned around the edges.

### Step 10

Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a **wire rack** to cool completely.

### Step 11

When cool, ice and decorate as desired.
