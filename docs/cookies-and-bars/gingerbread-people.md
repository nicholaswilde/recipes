# Gingerbread People

![Gingerbread People][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 2.20 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 1.5 cups dark molasses
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 0.67 cup cold water
- 0.33 cup vegetable shortening

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 cookie-cutters

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Mix dark molasses, brown sugar, cold water, and vegetable shortening. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

### Step 2

Preheat oven to 350°F.

### Step 3

Roll out dough and cut with cookie-cutters.

### Step 4

Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.

### Step 5

While they are still warm, spread evenly with lemon glaze.

### Step 6

Cool before frosting with Royal icing.
