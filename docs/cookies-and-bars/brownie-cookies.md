## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

bittersweet chocolate (chopped (112g))

### Step 2

unsalted butter ((56g))

### Step 3

granulated sugar ((100g))

### Step 4

packed light brown sugar ((110g))

### Step 5

eggs (room temperature)

### Step 6

vanilla extract

### Step 7

all-purpose flour ((80g))

### Step 8

unsweetened cocoa powder ((25g))

### Step 9

salt

### Step 10

baking powder

### Step 11

semisweet chocolate chips ((90g))

### Step 12

In a medium microwave-safe mixing bowl{}, combine the chocolate and butter. microwave on high in 20-second intervals,
stirring between each, until the chocolate and butter are melted, about 2 minutes.

### Step 13

In a large mixing bowl{}, beat the sugars and eggs on medium speed until light and frothy. Add the melted chocolate
mixture and vanilla and beat until combined. Add flour, cocoa powder, salt, and baking powder. Stir together with a
spatula until mostly combined. Stir in chocolate chips and mix until the batter is well combined. Cover the bowl and
chill the batter for 30 minutes.

### Step 14

Preheat the oven to 350°F Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.

### Step 15

Scoop the chilled dough into 2-tablespoon-sized balls and place on the prepared pans about 3 inches apart.

### Step 16

Bake for 8 to 10 minutes or until the cookies have spread and are shiny and cracked. (They will still be soft when hot
and firm up as they cool.) Let cool completely on the pans.
