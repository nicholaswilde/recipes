# :cookie: Almond Biscotti

![Almond Biscotti][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 3 dozen | 1.50 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :page_facing_up: some parchment paper
- :chestnut: 1 cup (142 g) whole raw almonds
- :bread: 2.25 cups (320 g) all-purpose flour
- :chestnut: 1.5 tsp baking powder
- :salt: 0.5 tsp salt
- :candy: 0.5 cup (99 g) granulated sugar
- :candy: 0.75 cup (160 g) brown sugar
- :egg: 3 large (150 g) eggs, room temperature
- :olive: 0.5 cup (100 g) olive oil
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 Tbsp pure almond extract
- :flower_playing_cards: 0.5 tsp pure vanilla extract
- :tangerine: 1 tsp lemon zest

## :cooking: Cookware

- :cookie: 1 baking sheet
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 medium bowl
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 large bowl
- :spoon: 1 wooden spoon
- :spoon: 1 large serving spoon
- :wood: 1 cutting board
- :knife: 1 serrated knife
- :wastebasket: 1 wire rack

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 325°F. Line a :cookie: baking sheet with :page_facing_up: parchment paper.

### Step 2

Spread :chestnut: whole raw almonds on the baking sheet and toast in the oven for 12 to 15 minutes. Let cool then coarsely chop.

### Step 3

In a :bowl_with_spoon: medium bowl, whisk together :bread: all-purpose flour, :chestnut: baking powder, :salt: salt, :candy: granulated sugar, and :candy: brown sugar.

### Step 4

In a :bowl_with_spoon: large bowl, whisk :egg: large eggs (at room temperature). Add :olive: olive oil, :flower_playing_cards: pure almond extract, :flower_playing_cards: pure vanilla extract, and
:tangerine: lemon zest. Whisk lightly together.

### Step 5

Add the flour mixture to the egg mixture; stir with a :spoon: wooden spoon until just incorporated.

### Step 6

Fold in the chopped almonds.

### Step 7

Use a :spoon: large serving spoon to scoop the dough onto the parchment-lined baking sheet, forming two logs (12 inches long).
With damp fingers, shape the logs.

### Step 8

Bake for 30 minutes until golden brown and firm. Let cool for 10 minutes.

### Step 9

Transfer logs to a :wood: cutting board. Using a :knife: serrated knife, slice into cookies at an angle (about 0.5 to 0.75 inch thick).

### Step 10

Place slices back on the baking sheets and bake for 15 to 20 minutes, turning them over halfway through.

### Step 11

Place on a :wastebasket: wire rack to cool.
