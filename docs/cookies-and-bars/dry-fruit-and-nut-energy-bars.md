# Dry Fruit and Nut Energy Bars

![Dry Fruit and Nut Energy Bars][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 14 bars | 25 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :grapes: 1 cup (149 g) dates
- :droplet: 1 cup (227 g) hot water
- :chestnut: 1 cup (113 g) cashews
- :chestnut: 1 cup (86 g) almonds
- :chestnut: 0.5 cup (64 g) walnuts
- :chestnut: 0.25 cup pistachios
- :seedling: 0.25 cup (36 g) sesame seeds
- :seedling: 0.25 cup (40 g) pumpkin seeds
- :coconut: 0.5 cup (113 g) dry coconut
- :honey_pot: 0.5 cup honey
- :seedling: 0.5 tsp cardamom powder
- :salt: 0.25 tsp salt
- :ear_of_rice: 0.5 cup (56 g) rolled oats

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 bowl
- 1 blender
- 1 pan
- 1 bowl
- 1 pan
- 1 pan
- 1 blender
- 1 plate
- 1 baking paper
- 1 airtight container

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Soak dates (pitted) in hot water for 2 hours in a bowl.

### Step 2

Blend the soaked dates to a smooth paste in a blender without adding any water, then keep aside.

### Step 3

In a pan on a low flame, roast cashews, almonds, walnuts, pistachios, sesame seeds, and pumpkin seeds until they turn
crunchy, about 5 minutes.

### Step 4

Add dry coconut (grated) and continue to roast, about 2 minutes. Transfer the roasted nuts and coconut to a bowl and
keep aside.

### Step 5

In the same pan, saute the dates paste on a medium flame until it thickens, about 5 minutes.

### Step 6

Add in the roasted nuts, honey, cardamom powder, and salt. Turn off the flame and mix well until combined.

### Step 7

In a separate pan on a medium flame, roast rolled oats until aromatic, about 3 minutes.

### Step 8

Blend the roasted oats to a fine powder in a blender.

### Step 9

Add the oats powder to the dry fruit and dates mixture. Combine well and keep mixing until the mixture thickens, about 2
minutes.

### Step 10

Transfer the prepared mixture into a greased plate lined with baking paper and set well forming a block, about 8
minutes.

### Step 11

Allow to set in the refrigerator for 1 hour.

### Step 12

Unmould and cut the block into 14 pieces. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up
to 1 month.
