# :candy: Butterscotch Squares

![Butterscotch Squares][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 4.12 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :olive: some nonstick cooking spray
- :candy: 2 cups brown sugar
- :glass_of_milk: 0.75 cup heavy whipping cream
- :butter: 6 tbsp unsalted butter
- :flower_playing_cards: 0.5 tsp vanilla
- :salt: 0.25 tsp salt
- :candy: 1 cup powdered sugar
- :chocolate_bar: 16 oz chocolate melts

## :cooking: Cookware

- :shallow_pan_of_food: 9x9" pan
- :page_facing_up: foil
- :shallow_pan_of_food: saucepan
- :page_facing_up: wax paper

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Line a :shallow_pan_of_food: 9x9" pan with :page_facing_up: foil and spray with :olive: nonstick cooking spray.

### Step 2

Combine :candy: brown sugar, :glass_of_milk: heavy whipping cream, and :butter: unsalted butter in a :shallow_pan_of_food: saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a rolling
boil.

### Step 3

Reduce heat to low and simmer until the mixture reaches 236ºF (7.5 minutes).

### Step 4

Remove from heat. Stir in :flower_playing_cards: vanilla, :salt: salt, and :candy: powdered sugar.

### Step 5

Pour into the prepared pan and let set at room temperature for 4 hours.

### Step 6

Cut into 64 small squares. Melt :chocolate_bar: chocolate melts. Dip each square into melted chocolate. Place on :page_facing_up: wax paper and chill
until set.
