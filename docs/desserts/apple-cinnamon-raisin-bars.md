# Apple Cinnamon Raisin Bars

![Apple Cinnamon Raisin Bars](../assets/images/apple-cinnamon-raisin-bars.jpg)

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 35 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1.5 tsp cinnamon
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 2 cups light brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 0.5 cup unsalted butter
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1.5 cup diced apples
- 0.75 cup raisins

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 9 X 13 pan
- 1 medium bowl
- 1 large bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Heat oven to 350Â°F. Coat a 9 X 13 pan. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt; set
aside.

### Step 2

In a large bowl, blend light brown sugar, eggs, unsalted butter, and vanilla.

### Step 3

Gradually add flour mixture. Stir in diced apples and raisins, and spread into pan.

### Step 4

Bake for 35 minutes.
