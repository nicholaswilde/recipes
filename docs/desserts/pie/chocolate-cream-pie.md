# :pie: Chocolate Cream Pie

![Chocolate Cream Pie][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 16 | 6 hours 20 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

### Oreo Pie Crust

- :cookie: 24 Oreo cookies
- :butter: 5 Tbsp (71 g) butter, melted

### Chocolate Pudding Filling

- :candy: 0.33 cup (66 g) granulated sugar
- :glass_of_milk: 2.5 cups (568 g) whole milk
- :egg: 6 large egg yolks
- :chestnut: 2 Tbsp cornstarch
- :butter: 6 Tbsp (85 g) salted butter, chopped
- :chocolate_bar: 8 oz semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
- :flower_playing_cards: 1.5 tsp vanilla extract

### Whipped Topping

- :glass_of_milk: 1 cup (227 g) heavy whipping cream
- :candy: 2 Tbsp (25 g) sugar
- :flower_playing_cards: 1.5 tsp vanilla extract

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 9-inch pie dish
- 1 saucepan

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Pulse Oreo cookies into crumbs. Mix with melted butter. Press into a 9-inch pie dish. Bake at 350°F for 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool.

### Step 2

Whisk granulated sugar and whole milk in a saucepan over medium heat until simmering.

### Step 3

Whisk large egg yolks and cornstarch, then slowly whisk in a few spoonfuls of the hot milk mixture before returning the entire egg mixture to the pan.

### Step 4

Whisk constantly until the mixture thickens and reaches a gentle boil, then remove from heat and stir in salted butter, semi-sweet chocolate, and vanilla extract until smooth.

### Step 5

Pour into the crust, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 4 to 6 hours.

### Step 6

Beat heavy whipping cream, sugar, and vanilla extract to soft peaks. Spread over the set pie and garnish with chocolate curls.
