# :candy: Copycat See's Bordeaux

![Copycat See's Bordeaux][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 2.37 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :butter: 8 tbsp unsalted butter
- :candy: 1 cup golden brown sugar
- :glass_of_milk: 0.25 cup heavy cream
- :coffee: 1 tsp instant coffee
- :salt: 0.13 tsp salt
- :candy: 2 cups powdered sugar
- :chestnut: 12 oz dark chocolate
- :butter: 1 tbsp butter

## :cooking: Cookware

- :shallow_pan_of_food: heavy saucepan
- :page_facing_up: wax paper
- :microwave: microwave

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Melt :butter: unsalted butter and :candy: golden brown sugar in a :shallow_pan_of_food: heavy saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a boil for 2 minutes.

### Step 2

Stir in :glass_of_milk: heavy cream, :coffee: instant coffee (if using), and :salt: salt. Boil for 30 seconds while stirring.

### Step 3

Remove from heat and cool for 10 minutes. Stir in :candy: powdered sugar to form a dough. Chill for 2 hours until firm.

### Step 4

Roll into 1-inch balls and place on :page_facing_up: wax paper. Freeze for 10 minutes.

### Step 5

Melt :chestnut: dark chocolate and :butter: butter in the :microwave: microwave at 50% power. Dip balls into chocolate and let set on :page_facing_up: wax paper.
