# German Chocolate Cake

![German Chocolate Cake][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 12 | 40 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 2 cups granulated sugar
- some all-purpose flour
- 0.75 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- some baking powder
- some baking soda
- 1 tsp salt
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup buttermilk
- 0.5 cup vegetable or canola oil
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup boiling water
- 0.5 cup light brown sugar
- 0.5 cup granulated sugar
- 0.5 cup butter
- 3 large egg yolks
- 0.75 cup evaporated milk
- 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 1 cup shredded sweetened coconut
- 0.5 cup butter
- 0.67 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
- 3 cups powdered sugar
- 0.33 cup evaporated milk
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 8 or 9-inch round baking pans
- 1 parchment paper
- 1 large bowl
- 1 wire racks
- 1 saucepan

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Grease and line two 8 or 9-inch round baking pans with parchment paper. Preheat the oven to 375°F.

### Step 2

### Step 3

In a large bowl, stir together granulated sugar (400 g), all-purpose flour (210 g), unsweetened cocoa powder (75 g),
baking powder (6 g), baking soda (7.5 g), and salt (5 g).

### Step 4

In a separate bowl, mix large eggs, buttermilk (240 ml), vegetable or canola oil (120 ml), and vanilla extract (10 ml).

### Step 5

Combine the wet and dry ingredients, then stir in boiling water (240 ml). (The batter will be thin.)

### Step 6

Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake for 25 to 35 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool for 5
minutes in the pans, then invert onto wire racks to cool completely.

### Step 7

### Step 8

In a saucepan, combine light brown sugar (100 g), granulated sugar (100 g), butter (115 g), large egg yolks, and
evaporated milk (180 ml). Bring to a low boil over medium heat, stirring constantly until thickened.

### Step 9

Remove from the heat and stir in vanilla extract (15 ml), chopped pecans (120 g), and shredded sweetened coconut (80 g).
Cool completely.

### Step 10

### Step 11

Melt butter (115 g) and stir in unsweetened cocoa powder (65 g).

### Step 12

Alternately add powdered sugar (360 g) and evaporated milk (80 ml), beating to a spreading consistency. Stir in vanilla
extract (5 ml).

### Step 13

### Step 14

Place one cake layer on a plate. Spread a thin layer of chocolate frosting, then half of the coconut frosting. Top with
the second cake layer. Cover the entire cake with chocolate frosting and spoon the remaining coconut frosting on top.
