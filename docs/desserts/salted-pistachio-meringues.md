# :cookie: Salted Pistachio Meringues

![Salted Pistachio Meringues][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 12 | 1.85 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :candy: 3 cups (570 g) superfine sugar
- :egg: 9 extra-large egg whites
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
- :chestnut: 0.33 cup (40 g) shelled, roasted, salted pistachios
- :salt: some fleur de sel

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 mixer

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 400°F. Line a sheet pan with parchment and spread superfine sugar over it. Bake for 10 minutes until
edges begin to brown. Lower oven to 250°F.

### Step 2

Whisk extra-large egg whites in an electric mixer with whisk attachment on high for 1 minute until frothy.

### Step 3

With mixer on low, slowly add the hot sugar. Add pure vanilla extract and whisk on high for 10 minutes until room
temperature and thick.

### Step 4

Line two sheet pans with parchment. Scoop 12 large dollops of meringue onto pans. Sprinkle with shelled, roasted, salted
pistachios (roughly chopped).

### Step 5

Bake for 1.5 hours at 250°F until crisp outside but soft inside.

### Step 6

Sprinkle with fleur de sel and cool.
