# :pie: Fresh Fruit Tart

![Fresh Fruit Tart][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1 9-inch tart | 3.50 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

### Pate Sucree (Sweet Tart Crust)
- :butter: 0.5 cup (113 g) unsalted butter, cool room temperature
- :candy: 0.25 cup (50 g) sugar
- :salt: 0.5 tsp kosher salt
- :bread: 1 cup (140 g) all-purpose flour
- :egg: 1 large (20 g) egg yolk, room temperature

### Pastry Cream
- :glass_of_milk: 1.66 cups (400 g) whole milk
- :candy: 0.75 cup (150 g) sugar
- :chestnut: 3 Tbsp (25 g) cornstarch
- :salt: 1 pinch salt
- :egg: 4 large (80 g) egg yolks
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp vanilla extract

### Assembly
- :glass_of_milk: 0.5 cup (120 g) heavy cream, whipped to stiff peaks
- :apple: 4 strawberries, halved
- :apple: 1 kiwi, sliced
- :apple: 1 Champagne mango, sliced
- :apple: 1 cup (150 g) blackberries
- :apple: 1 cup (120 g) raspberries
- :apple: 1 cup (170 g) blueberries

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: 1 stand mixer
- :shallow_pan_of_food: 1 9-inch tart pan
- :shallow_pan_of_food: 1 saucepan
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Pate Sucree (Sweet Tart Crust)

#### Step 1
In a :gear: stand mixer with a paddle attachment, cream :butter: unsalted butter, :candy: sugar, and :salt: kosher salt for 2 to 3
minutes until pale and light.

#### Step 2
Add :bread: all-purpose flour and mix on low for 30 seconds (will look like wet sand).

#### Step 3
Add :egg: egg yolk and mix until the dough just comes together (about 30 seconds).

#### Step 4
Wrap dough in plastic and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

#### Step 5
Preheat oven to 350°F. Roll out to 1/8-inch thickness, fit into a :shallow_pan_of_food: 9-inch tart pan, and trim edges. Freeze for
30 minutes.

#### Step 6
Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until golden brown. Let cool completely.

### Pastry Cream

#### Step 7
In a :shallow_pan_of_food: saucepan, heat :glass_of_milk: whole milk over medium-high until bubbles form around the edges.

#### Step 8
In a :bowl_with_spoon: bowl, mix :candy: sugar, :chestnut: cornstarch, and :salt: salt. Whisk in :egg: egg yolks until smooth.

#### Step 9
Slowly whisk the hot milk into the egg mixture to temper the eggs.

#### Step 10
Return the mixture to the saucepan. Whisk constantly over medium heat until it boils and thickens (about 1 to 2
minutes).

#### Step 11
Remove from heat, stir in :flower_playing_cards: vanilla extract, and strain into a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap (touching the
surface) and chill until cold.

### Assembly

#### Step 12
Gently fold :glass_of_milk: heavy cream (whipped to stiff peaks) into 1 cup of the chilled pastry cream until smooth.

#### Step 13
Spread the cream mixture evenly into the cooled tart shell.

#### Step 14
Arrange halved :apple: strawberries, sliced :apple: kiwi, sliced :apple: Champagne mango, :apple: blackberries, :apple: raspberries, and
:apple: blueberries over the cream.

#### Step 15
Serve within 6 hours of assembly.
