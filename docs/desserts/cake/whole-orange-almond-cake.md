# :tangerine: Whole Orange Almond Cake

![Whole Orange Almond Cake][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8 | 1.50 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :tangerine: 2 large oranges
- :egg: 6 eggs
- :chestnut: 1.5 cups (126 g) ground almonds
- :salt: 1 pinch salt
- :candy: 1 cup (198 g) sugar
- :chestnut: 1 teaspoon baking powder
- :tangerine: some thin slices of peeled orange
- :candy: some confectioners' sugar
- :chestnut: some cinnamon
- :strawberry: some fresh raspberries
- :icecream: some whipped cream

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 deep 9-inch cake pan
- 1 large pot
- 1 blender or food processor
- 1 bowl
- 1 rack
- 1 serving dish

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 400°F.

### Step 2

Butter and flour a deep 9-inch cake pan.

### Step 3

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.

### Step 4

Wash 2 large oranges (preferably seedless navels) and place them in the boiling water; cover the pot.

### Step 5

Boil until very soft, about 30 minutes.

### Step 6

Drain, cool, and cut into quarters, removing any seeds.

### Step 7

Process the oranges to a fairly fine puree in a blender or food processor (or use a meat grinder).

### Step 8

Beat 6 eggs in a bowl until thick.

### Step 9

Add 1.5 cups (126 g) ground almonds, 1 pinch salt, 1 cup (198 g) sugar, 1 teaspoon baking powder, and orange puree. Mix well.

### Step 10

Pour into the prepared cake pan and bake for 1 hour, or until the cake is firm to the touch when pressed with the tip of your finger.

### Step 11

Remove the pan to a rack and allow the cake to cool.

### Step 12

Turn it out of the pan into a serving dish.

### Step 13

Serve garnished with thin slices of peeled orange sprinkled with confectioners' sugar and a touch of cinnamon, or fresh
raspberries.

### Step 14

Serve with whipped cream.
