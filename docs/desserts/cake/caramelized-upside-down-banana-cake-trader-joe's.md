# :apple: Caramelized Upside Down Banana Cake Trader Joe's

![Caramelized Upside Down Banana Cake Trader Joe's][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8-10 | 1 hour |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :olive: some coconut oil spray
- :butter: 4 Tbsp (57 g) unsalted butter (melted)
- :candy: 0.5 cup (107 g) brown sugar
- :apple: 2 to 3 bananas (sliced)
- :bread: 1 box (425 g) Trader Joe's Banana Bread Mix
- :egg: 2 large eggs
- :olive: 0.33 cup (67 g) vegetable oil
- :droplet: 0.75 cup (170 g) water
- :apple: 1 banana (mashed)
- :glass_of_milk: some whipped cream

## :cooking: Cookware

- :shallow_pan_of_food: 1 pan
- :paintbrush: 1 pastry brush
- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 large bowl
- :cookie: 1 baking sheet
- :knife: 1 toothpick
- :shallow_pan_of_food: 1 cake plate

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray **pan** with **coconut oil spray**; set aside.

### Step 2

Pour **unsalted butter** (57 g) (melted) into the cake pan. Use a **pastry brush** to butter sides of pan. Evenly sprinkle **brown sugar** (107 g) over the butter.

### Step 3

Fan **bananas** (sliced) out from center of pan like spokes of a wheel; cover the entire pan bottom with banana slices. Use short pieces to fill in gaps. Stand short banana pieces around sides of pan (cut side in), spaced side by side; set pan aside.

### Step 4

In a **large bowl**, make batter with **Trader Joe's Banana Bread Mix** (425 g) according to pack directions (usually requires **eggs**, **vegetable oil** (67 g), and **water** (170 g)). Optional: Fold in **banana** (mashed) for extra flavor.

### Step 5

Place pan on **baking sheet** and gently pour batter over bananas.

### Step 6

Place on center rack of oven and bake for 55 to 65 minutes, or until center is set, golden brown, and springy to the touch. A **toothpick** inserted in the center should come out clean. Avoid having toothpick reach the gooey caramelized bananas.

### Step 7

Allow cake to cool for at least 1 hour before inverting onto a **cake plate**.

### Step 8

Serve with **whipped cream** if desired.
