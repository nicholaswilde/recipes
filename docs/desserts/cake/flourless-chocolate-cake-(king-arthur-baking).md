# Flourless Chocolate Cake (King Arthur Baking)

![Flourless Chocolate Cake (King Arthur Baking)][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8 | 0 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :chocolate_bar: 1 cup (170 g) chocolate chips
- :butter: 8 tablespoons (113 g) unsalted butter
- :candy: 0.75 cup (148 g) granulated sugar
- :salt: 0.25 teaspoon table salt
- :coffee: 1 teaspoon espresso powder
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- :egg: 3 large eggs
- :chocolate_bar: 0.5 cup Dutch-process cocoa powder
- :glass_of_milk: 0.5 cup (114 g) heavy cream
- :chocolate_bar: 1 cup (170 g) chocolate chips

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 oven
- 1 cake pan
- 1 bowl
- 1 bowl.
- 1 cake pan
- 1 bowl.

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat your oven to 375°F. Lightly grease an 8-inch round metal cake pan, and line the bottom with a circle of
parchment paper, then grease the parchment.

### Step 2

Combine chocolate chips and unsalted butter in a microwave-safe bowl (or saucepan) and melt on low heat, stirring until
completely smooth.

### Step 3

Transfer the melted mixture to a mixing bowl. Stir in granulated sugar, table salt, espresso powder, and vanilla
extract.

### Step 4

Whisk in large eggs one at a time, followed by Dutch-process cocoa powder. Mix until the batter is smooth and shiny.

### Step 5

Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan. Bake for 23 to 27 minutes, until the edges are firm and a thin crust has
formed on top. Let it cool completely.

### Step 6

To make the glaze, heat heavy cream until simmering, then pour it over chocolate chips in a bowl. Let sit for 3 minutes,
then stir until glossy and smooth. Pour the glaze over the cooled cake, spreading it evenly. Chill for at least 1 hour
before serving.
