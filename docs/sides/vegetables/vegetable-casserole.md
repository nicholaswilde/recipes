# :cucumber: Vegetable Casserole

![Vegetable Casserole][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 35 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 1 cup (121 g) zucchini
- :tomato: 1 large tomato
- :tea: 1 medium onion
- 0.33 cup [Parmesan cheese][2]
- :cheese_wedge: 0.5 cup reduced-fat [Bisquick](../../ingredients/bisquick.md)
- :coconut: 1 cup (227 g) fat-free milk
- 0.5 cup [egg substitute][3]
- :salt: some salt
- :salt: some pepper

## :cooking: Cookware

- :shallow_pan_of_food: 1 9-inch pie plate

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Heat oven to 400°F.

### Step 2

Grease a 9-inch pie plate.

### Step 3

Layer chopped zucchini, chopped tomato, onion, and Parmesan cheese. Stir reduced-fat
[Bisquick](../../ingredients/bisquick.md), fat-free milk, egg substitute, salt and pepper to taste until
blended, then pour into pie plate.

### Step 4

Bake for 35 minutes.
