# :broccoli: Steamed Broccoli with Vinaigrette

![Steamed Broccoli with Vinaigrette](../../assets/images/steamed-broccoli-with-vinaigrette.webp){ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 4 to 6 | 10 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :broccoli: 2 lbs broccoli
- :salt: 0.5 tsp salt
- some [vinaigrette][1]

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Steam broccoli until barely tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Season with salt.

### Step 2

Serve with vinaigrette.
