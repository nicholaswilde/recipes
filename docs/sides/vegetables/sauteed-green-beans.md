# :beans: Sauteed Green Beans

![Sauteed Green Beans](../../assets/images/sauteed-green-beans.webp){ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 4 | 6 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :butter: 2 Tbsp (28 g) unsalted butter
- :olive: 1 Tbsp (12 g) olive oil
- :garlic: 2 large garlic cloves
- :beans: 1 pound green beans
- :salt: some salt
- :salt: some black pepper
- :tangerine: 0.5 lemon

## :cooking: Cookware

- :shallow_pan_of_food: 1 large skillet

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Melt unsalted butter with olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add minced garlic cloves and swirl into the
butter mixture until fragrant, about 1 minute. You don't want the garlic to take on much color, just to become tender.

### Step 2

Add trimmed green beans and toss well, until they're coated in the butter and garlic mixture. Cover and cook for about 2
minutes (the beans will begin to steam and will brighten in color to a more lively green), then remove the pan from the
heat, uncover and toss again to ensure all the beans contact the bottom of the pan.

### Step 3

Return the covered pan to the heat and cook, tossing every so often, until the beans are tender and cooked through but
with a bit of a snap, about 3 minutes. Season with salt and black pepper and finish with a squeeze of lemon.
