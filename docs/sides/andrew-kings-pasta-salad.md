# Andrew King's Pasta Salad

![Andrew Kings Pasta Salad][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 10 | 0 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :spaghetti: 1 box Farfalle (bow tie) pasta
- :olive: 1 12-oz can black olives
- :falafel: 1 12-oz can garbanzo beans
- :cheese_wedge: 1 package mozzarella pearls
- :tomato: 1 container cherry tomatoes
- :deciduous_tree: 0.5 jar marinated artichoke hearts
- :hot_pepper: 0.5 jar sliced pepperoncinis
- :onion: 0.5 red onion (optional)
- :bell_pepper: 1 bell pepper (optional)
- :green_salad: 16 oz Light Olive Garden Dressing
- :leafy_green: 10 basil leaves
- :cheese_wedge: some shredded Parmesan
- :salt: some sea salt
- :salt: some fine black pepper

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 large bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Cook the Farfalle (bow tie) pasta according to package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water to cool.

### Step 2

In a large bowl, combine the cooled pasta, drained black olives, drained and rinsed garbanzo beans, mozzarella pearls,
and cherry tomatoes.

### Step 3

Add the marinated artichoke hearts and sliced pepperoncinis.

### Step 4

Add the finely chopped red onion (optional) and chopped bell pepper (optional) if using.

### Step 5

Pour the Light Olive Garden Dressing over the salad and toss to coat.

### Step 6

Stir in the julienned basil leaves.

### Step 7

Season with shredded Parmesan, sea salt, and fine black pepper to taste.
