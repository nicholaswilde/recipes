# :peanuts: Honey Roasted Peanuts

![Honey Roasted Peanuts][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 2 cups | 30 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :butter: 1.5 Tbsp unsalted butter
- :honey_pot: 1.5 Tbsp honey
- :salt: 0.25 tsp kosher salt
- :chestnut: 2 cups (284 g) raw unsalted peanuts
- :candy: 0.25 cup (50 g) white sugar
- :hot_pepper: 0.25 tsp chili powder
- :garlic: 0.25 tsp garlic powder
- :salt: 0.25 tsp kosher salt

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 oven
- 1 bowl
- 1 bowl
- 1 parchment-lined baking sheet
- 1 bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 350Â°F.

### Step 2

In a small microwave-safe bowl, melt the unsalted butter, honey, and kosher salt together.

### Step 3

Place the raw unsalted peanuts in a large bowl and pour the butter mixture over them. Toss to coat thoroughly.

### Step 4

Spread the peanuts in a single layer on a parchment-lined baking sheet.

### Step 5

Roast for 20 minutes, stirring halfway through, until golden brown.

### Step 6

Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together the white sugar, chili powder, garlic powder, and the remaining kosher salt.

### Step 7

Once the peanuts are done, remove them from the oven and immediately toss them in the sugar and spice mixture until well
coated.

### Step 8

Allow to cool completely before serving.
