# :chestnut: Rosemary Spiced Nuts

![Rosemary Spiced Nuts][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 10 | 20 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 1 lb (450 g) :chestnut: mixed nuts
- 1 Tbsp :butter: unsalted butter
- 1 Tbsp :honey_pot: maple syrup
- 2 Tbsp :herb: fresh rosemary
- 1 Tbsp :candy: dark brown sugar
- 0.5 tsp :hot_pepper: cayenne pepper
- 2 tsp :salt: flaky sea salt
- 1 tsp :herb: fresh rosemary
- :salt: flaky sea salt

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 :cookie: rimmed baking sheet
- 1 :bowl_with_spoon: large bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat your oven to 350. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.

### Step 2

In a large bowl, toss mixed nuts (cashews, walnuts, and almonds) with melted unsalted butter and maple syrup until
evenly coated.

### Step 3

Add 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh rosemary, the dark brown sugar, cayenne pepper, and 2 teaspoons flaky sea salt. Mix
thoroughly until the nuts are well coated with the spices and sugar.

### Step 4

Spread the nuts in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Bake until the nuts are golden, fragrant, and slightly
sticky 10 to 15 minutes, stirring once or twice to ensure even roasting and to prevent burning.

### Step 5

As soon as the nuts come out of the oven, sprinkle them with the remaining 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary and additional
flaky sea salt to taste.

### Step 6

Serve warm immediately, or let them cool completely and store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5
days.
