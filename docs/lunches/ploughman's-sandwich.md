# Ploughman's Sandwich

![Ploughman's Sandwich][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1 | 5 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 1 Tbsp butter
- 2 slices crusty bread
- 2 Tbsp [Branston Pickle Copycat](../sauces-and-dressings/gravy-and-savory-sauces/branston-pickle-copycat.md)
- 2 ounces aged cheddar cheese
- 1 small tomato
- 2 leaves lettuce

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 sandwich

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Spread butter (softened) evenly over one side of each slice of crusty bread (such as farmhouse white or rustic
sourdough).

### Step 2

On one slice of bread, spread a generous layer of [Branston Pickle Copycat](../sauces-and-dressings/gravy-and-savory-sauces/branston-pickle-copycat.md) over the butter.

### Step 3

Layer the aged cheddar cheese (sliced or crumbled), tomato (sliced), and crisp lettuce on top of the pickle, taking 5
minutes to assemble.

### Step 4

Top with the second slice of bread, buttered-side down. Press gently, cut the sandwich in half, and serve immediately.
