# :tomato: Sun-Dried Tomatoes

![Sun-Dried Tomatoes][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 6 | 5.50 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :apple: 3 cups (1008 g) cherry tomatoes
- :olive: some extra-virgin olive oil
- :salt: some sea salt
- :salt: some freshly ground black pepper
- :herb: some thyme sprigs

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 large baking sheet

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat the oven to 250°F. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.

### Step 2

Place the halved cherry tomatoes cut side up on the baking sheet.

### Step 3

Drizzle with extra-virgin olive oil and sprinkle with sea salt, freshly ground black pepper, and fresh thyme sprigs
(optional).

### Step 4

Roast the tomatoes for 2.5 hours to 3 hours until they are well shriveled around the edges.
