# :takeout_box: Soy Sauce

A traditional fermented condiment made from soybeans, roasted grain, brine, and Aspergillus oryzae or Aspergillus sojae molds.

## :timer_clock: Storage
- Store in a cool, dark place.
- Refrigeration can help maintain flavor for longer periods.
