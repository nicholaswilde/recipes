# :ear_of_rice: Toasted Rolled Oats

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 10 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :ear_of_rice: some rolled oats

## :cooking: Cookware

- :cookie: 1 rimmed baking sheet

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350°F. Spread rolled oats in a single layer on a rimmed baking sheet.

### Step 2

Bake for 10 minutes, stirring and mixing halfway through to ensure even toasting. The oats are done when they are
golden brown and fragrant.

### Step 3

Remove from the oven and let cool completely before using.

!!! tip

    Keep a close eye on the oats during the last few minutes as they can go from golden to burnt quickly.
