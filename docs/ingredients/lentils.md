# :beans: Lentils

Lentils are small, lens-shaped legumes available in various colors and textures.

## Varieties
- **Brown Lentils:** Hold their shape well, earthy flavor.
- **Green Lentils:** Firm texture, peppery flavor.
- **Red/Yellow Lentils:** Break down when cooked, great for thickening soups and dals.
- **Black (Beluga) Lentils:** Small, firm, and shiny.

## :timer_clock: Storage
- Store dried lentils in a cool, dry place for up to a year.
- Cooked lentils last 3-5 days in the refrigerator.
