# :chestnut: Sweet Tart

![Sweet Tart](../../assets/images/sweet-tart.jpg){ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 1 hour 15 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :chestnut: 40 g [almond flour][1]
- :candy: 60 g confectioners' sugar
- :bread: 250 g all-purpose flour
- :salt: 1 pinch salt
- :butter: 150 g cold butter
- :egg: 1 egg

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 sheet pan
- 1 food processor
- 1 plastic wrap
- 1 rolling pin
- 1 9-inch tart ring or pan

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F. Toast [almond flour][1] in sheet pan until fragrant, about 5 to 10 minutes.

### Step 2

Let the [almond flour][1] cool completely.

### Step 3

Add the confectioners' sugar, all-purpose flour, salt, and [almond flour][1] to a food processor and pulse.

### Step 4

Add the cold butter and pulse until the mixture looks like breadcrumbs.

### Step 5

Add the egg and pulse until the dough just comes together.

### Step 6

Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

### Step 7

On a floured surface, roll out the dough to 3mm thickness.

### Step 8

Line a 9-inch tart ring or pan with the dough.

### Step 9

Prick the bottom with a fork and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

### Step 10

Bake at 350°F for 15 to 20 minutes or until golden brown.
