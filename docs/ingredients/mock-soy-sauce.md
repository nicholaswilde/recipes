# :stew: Mock Soy Sauce

![Mock Soy Sauce][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Yields | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1 cup | 25 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :stew: 2 cups (368 g) (475 ml) [Vegetable Broth](vegetable-broth.md) or beef broth
- :apple: 2 Tbsp (30 ml) balsamic vinegar
- :apple: 1 Tbsp (18 g) (15 ml) apple cider vinegar
- :chestnut: 1 Tbsp (21 g) dark molasses
- :salt: 1 tsp table salt
- :garlic: 1 pinch garlic powder
- :sweet_potato: 1 pinch ground ginger

## :cooking: Cookware

- :shallow_pan_of_food: small saucepan
- whisk
- glass jar

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Combine the **broth**, **balsamic vinegar**, **apple cider vinegar**, **molasses**, **salt**, **garlic powder**, and **ginger** in a small saucepan.

### Step 2

Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.

### Step 3

Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium and simmer uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the liquid has reduced by half (to about 1 cup).

### Step 4

Remove from heat and let it cool completely.

### Step 5

Transfer to a glass jar and store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
