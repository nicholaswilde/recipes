# Beau Monde Seasoning

![Beau Monde Seasoning][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1 | 0 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :seedling: 2 Tbsp celery seeds
- :onion: 2 Tbsp granulated onion
- :salt: 2 Tbsp kosher salt
- :candy: 1 tsp granulated sugar

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 spice grinder
- 1 mortar and pestle
- 1 airtight container

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Place celery seeds, granulated onion, kosher salt, and granulated sugar in a spice grinder and process until finely
ground and powdery (about 30 seconds).

### Step 2

Alternatively, you can also use a mortar and pestle to pound and grind the ingredients into a fine powder.

### Step 3

Store the seasoning in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 6 months.
