# Self-Rising Cornmeal

![Self-Rising Cornmeal][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 2 cups | 5 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :ear_of_rice: 1.75 cups (242 g) cornmeal
- :bread: 6 Tbsp (45 g) all-purpose flour
- :chestnut: 2 Tbsp (24 g) baking powder
- :salt: 0.5 tsp (3 g) salt

## :cooking: Cookware

- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Blend cornmeal, all-purpose flour, baking powder, and salt together in a bowl.

### Step 2

Use according to whatever recipe you are following.
