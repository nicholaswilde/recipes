# Espresso Powder

![Espresso Powder][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1 cup | 1.08 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :coffee: 1 cup (85 g) espresso beans

## :cooking: Cookware

- :cookie: 1 baking sheet
- :gear: 1 coffee or spice grinder
- :package: 1 container with a tight-fitting lid

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat oven to 200Â°F.

### Step 2

Spread espresso beans in a single layer onto a baking sheet. Bake until lightly toasted and dried, about 1 hour.

### Step 3

Remove from the oven and grind in batches in a coffee or spice grinder until finely ground.

### Step 4

Pour into a container with a tight-fitting lid (such as a small Mason jar or spice jar) and store for up to 6 months.
