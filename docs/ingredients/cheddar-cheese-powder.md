# :cheese_wedge: Cheddar Cheese Powder

![Cheddar Cheese Powder][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1 cup | 18.00 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :cheese_wedge: 8 oz (114 g) cheddar cheese

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 dehydrator trays
- 1 parchment paper
- 1 paper towels
- 1 blender
- 1 airtight jar

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Grate cheddar cheese as finely as possible.

### Step 2

Spread the grated cheese in a thin, even layer on dehydrator trays lined with parchment paper. Set the temperature to
125 °F (or lowest setting).

### Step 3

Dehydrate the cheese, checking every few hours to gently pat with paper towels to absorb any excess oil released by the
cheese. Dehydrate for 12 to 18 hours until the cheese is completely dry, brittle, and snaps easily. Allow to cool
completely.

### Step 4

Transfer the dried cheese to a blender or food processor and pulse until it reaches a fine, powdery consistency.

### Step 5

Store in an airtight jar in a cool, dry place.
