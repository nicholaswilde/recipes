# Self-Rising Flour

![Self-Rising Flour][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Yields | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1 cup | 5 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :bread: 1 cup (120 g) all-purpose flour
- :chestnut: 1 1/2 tsp (6 g) [baking powder][2]
- :salt: 1/4 tsp (1.5 g) table salt

## :cooking: Cookware

- small bowl
- whisk
- sifter
- airtight container

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Measure out the **all-purpose flour**, **baking powder**, and **table salt**.

### Step 2

Whisk the ingredients together in a small bowl until well combined.

### Step 3

Sift the mixture at least twice to ensure the baking powder and salt are evenly distributed throughout the flour. This is crucial for an even rise in your baked goods.

### Step 4

Transfer to an airtight container. Store in a cool, dry place for up to 6 months.

### Step 5

**Note:** If a recipe calls for self-rising flour and you use this homemade version, do not add the baking powder or salt already listed in that recipe unless specifically instructed.
