# Baking Powder

![Baking Powder][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Yields | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 1/4 cup | 5 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :chestnut: 2 Tbsp (18 g) cream of tartar
- :chestnut: 1 Tbsp (18 g) baking soda
- :chestnut: 1 Tbsp (7 g) cornstarch (optional, for storage)

## :cooking: Cookware

- small bowl or sifter
- whisk
- airtight container

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Measure out the **cream of tartar**, **baking soda**, and **cornstarch** (if using).

### Step 2

Sift the ingredients together into a small bowl or whisk them thoroughly to ensure they are completely combined and there are no clumps.

### Step 3

If using immediately, you can omit the **cornstarch**. If storing, the **cornstarch** acts as an anti-caking agent to prevent clumping and premature reaction.

### Step 4

Transfer to an airtight container. Store in a cool, dry place for up to 6 months.

### Step 5

**Note:** This is single-acting baking powder. Once added to liquid, it begins to react immediately, so get your batter into the oven as quickly as possible to ensure a proper rise.
