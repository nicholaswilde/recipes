# :lemon: Lemon Glaze

![Lemon Glaze](../../assets/images/lemon-glaze.webp){ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 10 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :baby_bottle: 1.25 cups (141 g) confectioners' sugar, sifted
- :tangerine: 0.25 cup fresh lemon, orange, or lime juice
- :flower_playing_cards: 1 tsp vanilla

## :cooking: Cookware

## :pencil: Instructions

This glaze should be spread on warm cakes or Christmas cookies. It has a fine consistency for embedding decorative nuts
and fruits.

### Step 1

Beat together until smooth confectioners' sugar, sifted, fresh lemon, orange, or lime juice, and vanilla.
