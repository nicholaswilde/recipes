# Watermelon Agua Fresca

![Watermelon Agua Fresca][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 2 quarts | 10 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :baby_bottle: 1 each mini watermelon
- :baby_bottle: 2.5 lbs diced watermelon
- :baby_bottle: 0.5 cup (168 g) sugar or agave syrup
- :droplet: 1 quart water

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 blender
- 1 fine strainer

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Combine peeled and diced mini watermelon (or diced watermelon) and sugar or agave syrup in a blender.

### Step 2

Blend until completely smooth, about 2 minutes.

### Step 3

Strain the mixture through a fine strainer to remove any pulp, about 5 minutes.

### Step 4

Add cold water and whisk until well combined, about 3 minutes.
