# :strawberry: Strawberry Whip

![Strawberry Whip][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 2 | 5 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :strawberry: 2 cups (334 g) strawberries
- :candy: 0.25 cup (50 g) granulated sugar
- :glass_of_milk: 1 cup vanilla yogurt
- :tangerine: 0.5 cup (120 g) pineapple juice
- :baby_bottle: 1 cup (142 g) crushed ice

## :cooking: Cookware

- :gear: 1 blender or food processor

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Sprinkle strawberries with granulated sugar.

### Step 2

Place in blender or food processor with vanilla yogurt and pineapple juice. Blend well.

### Step 3

Add crushed ice. Blend.

### Step 4

Makes two generous, frosty drinks.
