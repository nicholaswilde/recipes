# :clinking_glasses: Champagne Punch

![Champagne Punch][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 5 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 4 cups Kern's apricot nectar
- :tangerine: 4 cups (964 g) pineapple juice
- 4 cups (1600 g) guava nectar
- :tumbler_glass: 2 cups (426 g) light rum
- :ice_cream: some ice cubes or mold
- :apple: 6 cups chilled champagne

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 punch bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Mix Kern's apricot nectar, pineapple juice, guava nectar, light rum, ice cubes or mold, and chilled champagne in a punch
bowl.

### Step 2

Enjoy!
