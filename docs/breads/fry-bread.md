# Fry Bread

![Fry Bread][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8 | 2.5 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- 4 cups (480 g) :bread: all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp (6 g) :salt: salt
- 1.5 Tbsp (18 g) :chestnut: baking powder
- 1.5 cups (340 g) :droplet: hot water (120°F to 130°F)
- 2 Tbsp (25 g) :olive: vegetable oil
- some :olive: vegetable oil (for frying)

## :cooking: Cookware

- :bowl_with_spoon: mixing bowl
- :shallow_pan_of_food: pot

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

In a large :bowl_with_spoon: mixing bowl, stir together the all-purpose flour, salt, and baking powder.

### Step 2

Gradually add the hot water (120°F to 130°F) to the dry ingredients. Mix with a spoon or your hands until the dough is
combined.

### Step 3

Drizzle the vegetable oil over the dough to prevent it from drying out. Cover the bowl with a clean towel or plastic
wrap and let it rest for ~{2 hours}.

### Step 4

Pour enough vegetable oil (for frying) into a :shallow_pan_of_food: pot to reach a depth of about 2 inches. Heat the oil to 350°F.

### Step 5

Pull off a golf-ball-sized piece of dough. Pat or roll it out into a flat circle (about 0.25 to 0.5 inch thick).

### Step 6

Carefully place the dough into the hot oil. Fry for 1 to ~{2 minutes} per side until golden brown and puffed. Work with one
piece at a time to maintain the oil temperature.

### Step 7

Remove the bread and place it on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
