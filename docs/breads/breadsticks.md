# :bread: Breadsticks

![Breadsticks][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 9 | 2 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

- :droplet: 0.75 cup (170 g) warm water
- :tea: 2.25 tsp active dry yeast
- :candy: 1 pinch sugar
- :olive: 2 Tbsp (25 g) olive oil
- :salt: 1 tsp salt
- :garlic: 0.25 tsp garlic powder (optional)
- :bread: 1 cup (120 g) bread flour
- :bread: 1.33 cups (160 g) bread flour
- :butter: 3 Tbsp (43 g) unsalted butter
- :salt: 0.25 tsp salt
- :garlic: 0.25 tsp garlic powder
- :herb: 1 tsp dried parsley
- :cheese_wedge: some grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

## :cooking: Cookware

- 1 large bowl
- 1 lightly greased bowl
- 1 parchment-lined baking sheet

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Combine warm water, active dry yeast, and a pinch of sugar in a large bowl. Let sit for 5 to 10 minutes until foamy.

### Step 2

Stir in the remaining sugar (total 1.5 Tbsp), olive oil, salt, garlic powder (optional), and 1 cup (125g) of bread flour
until combined.

### Step 3

Gradually add remaining bread flour until dough is tacky but manageable.

### Step 4

Knead on a lightly floured surface for 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.

### Step 5

Place dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm spot until doubled, 45 to 60 minutes.

### Step 6

Deflate dough, divide into 9 pieces, and form into 6-7” ropes.

### Step 7

Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet, cover, and let rise until increased by 50%, about 20 minutes.

### Step 8

Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C).

### Step 9

Bake for 10 to 13 minutes until light golden brown.

### Step 10

Whisk together melted unsalted butter, salt, garlic powder, and dried parsley for the topping.

### Step 11

Brush topping over warm breadsticks and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese (optional) if desired.

### Step 12

Serve warm.
