# Beautiful Burger Buns

![Beautiful Burger Buns][1]{ loading=lazy }

| :fork_and_knife_with_plate: Serves | :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:----------------------------------:|:-----------------------: |
| 8 buns | 2.5 to 3.5 hours |

## :salt: Ingredients

### Buns
- :bread: 3 1/2 cups (420 g) King Arthur Unbleached All-Purpose Flour
- :droplet: 3/4 cup plus 2 Tbsp (198 g) water, lukewarm
- :butter: 2 Tbsp (28 g) butter, at room temperature
- :egg: 1 large egg
- :candy: 1/4 cup (50 g) granulated sugar
- :salt: 1 1/4 tsp table salt
- :tea: 1 Tbsp (9 g) instant yeast

### Butter glaze
- :butter: 3 Tbsp (43 g) butter, melted; divided

### Seed topping (optional)
- :egg: 1 large egg white, whisked with 2 Tbsp cold water
- :seedling: Sesame seeds, or the seeds of your choice

## :cooking: Cookware

- large bowl or stand mixer
- baking sheet
- parchment paper (optional)
- rack

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Mix and knead all of the dough ingredients — by hand, mixer, or bread machine — to make a soft, smooth dough.

### Step 2

Cover the dough and let it rise until it's nearly doubled in bulk, about 1 to 2 hours.

### Step 3

Gently deflate the dough and divide it into eight pieces (about 100 g each). Shape each piece into a ball.

### Step 4

Flatten each dough ball with the palm of your hand until it's about 3" in diameter.

### Step 5

Place the buns on a lightly greased or parchment-lined baking sheet. Cover and let rise until noticeably puffy, about an hour. Toward the end of the rising time, preheat the oven to 375°F.

### Step 6

Brush the buns with about half of the melted butter. To make seeded buns, brush the egg white/water mixture right over the melted butter and sprinkle with seeds.

### Step 7

Bake for 15 to 18 minutes, until golden. Remove from the oven and brush with the remaining melted butter for a satiny crust.

### Step 8

Cool on a rack before slicing horizontally.
