# :baguette_bread: Garlic Bread

![Garlic Bread](../assets/images/garlic-bread.webp){ loading=lazy }

| :timer_clock: Total Time |
|:-----------------------: |
| 2 minutes |

## :salt: Ingredients

=== "serves 3-4"

    - :baby_bottle: 1 sticks butter, softened
    - :garlic: 1 cloves garlic, minced
    - :tea: 0.5 scallion, minced
    - :cheese_wedge: 0.5 Tbsp (3 g) Parmesan
    - :herb: 0.5 Tbsp parsley
    - :droplet: some hot sauce
    - :salt: some salt
    - :salt: some pepper
    - :baguette_bread: 1 French baguette

=== "serves 6-8"

    - :baby_bottle: 2 sticks butter, softened
    - :garlic: 2 cloves garlic, minced
    - :tea: 1 scallion, minced
    - :cheese_wedge: 1 Tbsp (6 g) Parmesan
    - :herb: 1 Tbsp parsley
    - :droplet: some hot sauce
    - :salt: some salt
    - :salt: some pepper
    - :baguette_bread: 1 French baguette

## :cooking: Cookware

- :bowl_with_spoon: 1 small bowl

## :pencil: Instructions

### Step 1

Preheat broiler with rack 4 inches from heat.

### Step 2

In a small bowl, mix butter, softened, together with garlic, minced, scallion, minced, Parmesan, parsley, hot sauce to
taste and salt and pepper, to taste.

### Step 3

Slice French baguette horizontally.

### Step 4

Spread butter on both halves.

### Step 5

Place on a sheet pan and toast under the broiler, about 2 minutes.
